# Lead Trust

Trust is the reason JobFilter is not a generic job board. Leads must be real, structured, sourced, and useful.

## Rules
- No fake leads.
- No empty outputs.
- Prefer official APIs and compliant public sources.
- Expose source and source confidence on every lead.
