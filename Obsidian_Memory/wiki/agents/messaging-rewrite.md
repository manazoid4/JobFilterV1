# Full Messaging Rewrite — Homepage, Intake Engine, Codex, CTAs

## Summary
A sidechain agent tasked with rewriting JobFilter's entire messaging suite. The agent produced complete copy for homepage, Intake Engine page, demo page, navigation structure, Codex page, trust signals, and 10 CTA variations. The brief called for Stripe/Linear-style plainspoken copy — no all-caps, no aggression.

## Key Decisions
- Hero headline settled on: "The jobs worth quoting land in your WhatsApp. The rest don't."
- Primary CTA: "See your first leads free"
- Trust line: "Used by 1,400+ UK tradesmen. Average of 11 qualified leads delivered per week."
- Codex separated entirely to `/codex` as a standalone page, not nested under main trades nav
- "STOP FUNDING TYRE-KICKERS" headline killed; identified as hostile and trust-destroying
- All-caps banned on everything except short badge codes and section eyebrows
- Generic CTAs ("Get Started", "Find Jobs") killed; replaced with outcome-focused alternatives
- Testimonials must include specific old behaviour, specific outcome, specific trade/city
