# JobFilter Growth Ideas — Adapted for Fitrah

The proven model behind JobFilter, rebuilt for a health brand.

---

## 1. Hook → Scan → Pay (Adapted)

### JobFilter version:
User enters postcode → scans for leads → pays to unlock Gold leads.

### Fitrah version:
User takes quiz → gets their mineral/hydration score → pays to access full protocol or first batch.

**The quiz IS the scan.** It qualifies the customer and segments them. A man who scores "significantly under-fuelled" is the hottest lead. A man who scores "well hydrated" needs a different CTA (product angle vs. basic education).

**Build:** Typeform or Tally (free) → results page → email capture → Mailchimp → sequence.

---

## 2. Diagnostic Free Tool = Lead Magnet

### JobFilter version:
Free scan of jobs near you. You see the results. Paid tier unlocks full detail.

### Fitrah version:
- Mineral Deficiency Risk Checker
- Hydration Quality Score
- Training Fuel Checker
- Testosterone Lifestyle Score

User gets real value for free. They also give their email, tell you their biggest problems, and self-segment into different buyer types.

**Build:** Start with one quiz (Mineral Deficiency Risk Checker). Keep it to 8 questions. Link result to email capture and waitlist.

---

## 3. Founder 100 (Same Principle)

### JobFilter version:
Founding 30 — first 30 subscribers get locked-in price, PatchLock, priority access.

### Fitrah version:
**Founder 50** — first 50 people get:
- Locked founder price (£10–£15 below future retail)
- First batch guaranteed
- Behind-the-scenes access
- Private WhatsApp / community
- Recipe drops
- Founder badge on future packaging
- Voice in the formulation (taste feedback sessions)

**Price to join:** £29 pre-order credit OR £49 Founder Challenge.  
**Why it works:** Early supporters feel ownership. They market for you. They tolerate minor product iteration.

---

## 4. Scarcity (Real, Not Fake)

### JobFilter version:
"Founding 30 locks price. Patch control locks value." — genuine capacity constraints.

### Fitrah version:
- First batch limited by kitchen capacity (genuinely true)
- Founder 50 hard cap (genuinely true — you only want manageable feedback)
- First 20 Founder Challenge places (genuinely limited by your time)

**Never use:** "Only 3 left" if that's not true. "Timer ends tonight" if you'll reopen tomorrow.

---

## 5. Paid Dashboard / Community (Adapted)

### JobFilter version:
Paid dashboard → lead tracking, territory, pipeline, Admin Guard.

### Fitrah version:
**Paid Member Hub** (Month 3+):
- Private community (WhatsApp group initially → Circle or Skool later)
- Monthly mineral reset challenges
- Exclusive recipes
- Training fuel plans
- Behind-the-scenes formulation
- First access to new products
- Monthly Q&A with founder

**Price:** £9–£15/month.  
**Value:** Sense of belonging + exclusive content + founder access.

---

## 6. Territory Idea — Local Gym/Community Anchor

### JobFilter version:
PatchLock — one tradesperson locks one area, gets priority leads there.

### Fitrah version:
**Gym Anchor Program** — one gym in each city gets exclusive Fitrah sampling rights.
- Fitrah supplies free samples to that gym
- Gym recommends to members
- Gym gets a revenue share or co-branding benefit
- City is "Fitrah's gym in [City]"

**Why it works:** Gives gym owners a reason to promote. Gives Fitrah distribution without ad spend. Creates real local proof.

---

## 7. Free Tools = Lead Flow Engine

### JobFilter version:
Free lead scan → leads drive to paid → email capture at every stage.

### Fitrah version:
Every piece of content drives to a free tool.  
Every free tool captures an email.  
Every email goes into a sequence that drives to a purchase.

Content → Quiz → Email → Offer.  
That's the whole funnel. Keep it simple.

---

## 8. Content Funnel (Founder Journey)

### JobFilter version:
Build in public → show product working → proof → convert.

### Fitrah version:
**The founder journey IS the content:**
- "I'm building a real mineral drink. Here's why." (day 1)
- "Taste test — first formula attempt" (week 1)
- "Here's what's actually in most pre-workouts (and why I'm not using any of it)" (week 2)
- "Founder 50 now open — here's what you get" (week 3)
- "First batch arrived. This is what it looks like." (month 2)
- "First 10 reviews — honest feedback" (month 2)

People buy from founders they trust. Show the work. Show the reasoning. Show the real product being built.

---

## 9. Productised Service (Before Physical Product)

### JobFilter version:
Tools, dashboards, and scored leads are all productised.

### Fitrah version:
Sell information products before physical products:
- £9 Mineral Guide — pure digital, zero cost to produce
- £19 7-Day Protocol — digital, zero cost
- £29 Starter Pack — digital bundle

This is a productised service. It generates cash before you spend on manufacturing. It proves people will pay for your knowledge and approach. If nobody buys the guide, reconsider before spending £5,000 on a first production run.

---

## 10. Upsell Ladder

### JobFilter version:
Free scan → paid membership → PatchLock → add-ons → premium bundles.

### Fitrah version:
Quiz → email → £9 guide → £19 protocol → £49 challenge → £29/month subscription → £99 transformation pack → referral/community.

Every offer points to the next.  
Make it easy to say yes at every level.  
Never push the £99 before someone's bought the £9.

---

## What NOT to Copy From JobFilter

| JobFilter | Don't copy for Fitrah |
|---|---|
| WhatsApp lead delivery | WhatsApp is powerful but Fitrah's community comes first, not alerts |
| Complex lead scoring engine | Not needed — product is simple |
| Multiple fetchers / API sources | Not relevant |
| Territory system | Gym Anchor is simpler and better |
| Subscription complexity | Start with flat monthly — no patch add-ons yet |
