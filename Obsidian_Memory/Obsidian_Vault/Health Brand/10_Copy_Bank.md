## Faith-Compatible Copy Lines (Non-Preachy)

Use these for the Muslim audience without alienating others:

- "Halal-conscious. Made with ingredients you can trust."
- "Real food. Clean conscience."
- "No haram ingredients. No shortcuts."
- "The pomegranate has been celebrated for centuries. We put it in a bottle."
- "Discipline is a daily practice. So is proper hydration."
- "A drink that fits your lifestyle — not just your macros."

Do NOT use: "Islamic drink", "Sunnah-approved", "Prophet's food" (medical/religious claim territory), or anything preachy.
