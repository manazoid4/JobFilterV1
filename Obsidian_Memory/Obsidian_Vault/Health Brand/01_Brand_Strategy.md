# Brand Strategy

---

## Mission

Real nourishment for men rebuilding strength, discipline and energy.

Not a gym supplement brand.  
Not a lifestyle brand for the soft.  
A food-first, mineral-rich brand for men who take their body seriously — and live with intention.

---

## Brand Enemy

**Ultra-processed everything.**

- Neon pre-workouts with 47 ingredients
- Seed oil food dressed up as "healthy"
- Testosterone booster scams
- Protein bars made in a lab
- Energy drinks that crash you at 3pm
- Soft wellness brands that make you feel like you're buying a spa day
- Brands that sell discipline without believing in it

---

## Brand Promise

You don't need a stack of synthetic supplements.  
You need minerals, roots, salt, real food and a reason to train.

We make the drink. You do the work.

---

## Core Beliefs

1. Real food beats fake supplements.
2. Minerals matter more than most men realise.
3. Discipline is a practice, not a product.
4. Halal living and peak performance are not in conflict.
5. Your grandfather didn't need creatine and he built things.
6. Pomegranate, beetroot, salt and lemon existed before the supplement industry.
7. If you can't read the ingredient list, it's not food.

---

## Target Customer (Primary)

Muslim man, 22–40, UK.  
Rebuilding his health, diet and discipline post-bad habits.  
Trains or wants to train.  
Aware of halal food but doesn't want just halal junk.  
Wants real food, real energy, real results.  
Tired of fake supplements. Interested in whole foods.  
Faith matters to him but he's not looking for lectures.

---

## Target Customer (Secondary)

Non-Muslim gym man, 25–40, UK.  
Done with fake pre-workouts.  
Interested in ancestral foods, beef tallow, raw milk, real nutrition.  
Might follow Dr Paul Saladino, Liver King (before the fall), or similar.  
Wants something clean and masculine — not pastel wellness.

---

## Target Customer (Tertiary)

Blue-collar tradesman, 28–45, UK.  
Long days, physical work.  
Currently drinks energy drinks or coffee.  
Would switch if given something real that works and isn't complicated.  
Doesn't care about aesthetics — cares about energy and simplicity.

## Brand Tone

- Direct. Not shouty.
- Confident. Not arrogant.
- Faith-compatible. Not preachy.
- Masculine. Not cringe.
- Grounded. Not mystical.
- Simple. Not dumbed down.
- Honest. Not fake.

---

## What We Never Do

- Fake testosterone claims
- Medical claims of any kind
- Fake social proof
- Haram ingredients
- Alcohol-based extracts
- Fake scarcity
- Paid influencers before the product is real
- Overbuild before validating
