# Service Manager Persona

Updated: 6 May 2026

## Buyer

Service Manager / Head of Customer Operations at a telecom, broadband, managed Wi-Fi, or network services company.

Example context: a Dublin-based telecom-style business with service desk, complaints, finance, field ops, and account management teams.

## Day-to-day pain

- Broadband faults with unclear root cause
- Business customers shouting about downtime
- Billing disputes after package changes or failed provisioning
- SLA clocks running while teams argue ownership
- Field engineers missing notes, parts, access, or handover detail
- Repeat tickets from the same customer
- Finance asking whether a credit is valid
- Account managers warning that a customer is ready to leave

## Core Problem

They do not lack tools.

They lack clean operational truth.

They need fast answers to:

- What is actually at risk today?
- Which customers need intervention before they churn?
- Which complaints are caused by billing, network, engineer delay, or bad handoff?
- Which SLA breaches are preventable?
- Which teams keep creating downstream work?
- Which accounts are costing more than they are worth?

## AgentDock Position

AgentDock is the control layer for service operations.

Not another ticketing system.
Not another dashboard.

A way to spot the jobs, complaints, customers, and handoffs that are about to cost money.

## Copy Hooks

- Stop finding service problems after the customer has already lost trust.
- Know which tickets are about to cost you money.
- SLA risk, complaint risk, churn risk. One clean view.
- Your service team does not need more tickets. It needs better signals.
- Protect revenue before service failures become credits, complaints, or churn.
