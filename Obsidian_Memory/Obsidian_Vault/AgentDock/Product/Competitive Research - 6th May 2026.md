# Competitive Research - 6th May 2026

## Summary

Competitors prove demand. Current repo research now covers 42 competitors across ITSM, CX AI, contact centre AI, RPA, workflow automation, and agent frameworks.

Most fall into two buckets:

- Enterprise suites that automate inside their own ecosystem.
- Agent builders that require technical teams to create an operations product.

AgentDock should own the middle:

```text
Builders create agents.
Suites automate inside one ecosystem.
AgentDock shows service managers what agents are doing across departments.
```

## AgentDock Gap

Lead with:

```text
AI service agents without replacing your service desk.
```

Then prove:

- Complaint risk
- Billing disputes
- SLA threats
- Field handoff quality
- Churn warning signals
- Human approval before sensitive action
- Audit trails for what changed and why

## Site Direction

Use a calm 2D office control floor:

- Service Desk
- Complaints
- Finance
- Field Ops
- Meeting Room
- Kitchen / paused work
- Review Gate

The visual metaphor shows where agent work is happening and where it needs a human decision.
