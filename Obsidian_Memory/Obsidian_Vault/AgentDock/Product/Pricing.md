## Business Tier Details

- Up to 5 connected systems (ServiceNow, Zendesk, Jira, email, webhooks)
- 15 active agents across all workflows
- All pipeline templates:
  - Complaint management
  - Ticket triage & routing
  - Response drafting + approval
  - Escalation handling
  - Report generation
  - Data sync between systems
- Full audit log (1 year retention)
- Compliance mode — all actions logged, approval required for critical actions
- Human approval gates before sending, updating, or deleting records
- Basic role-based access (admin, operator, viewer)

## Enterprise Details

Everything in Business, plus:

- Unlimited connected systems
- Unlimited agents
- Custom pipeline templates built for their workflows
- Unlimited audit log with export (CSV, PDF)
- Custom compliance rules (GDPR, HIPAA, SOC 2 alignment)
- SSO / SAML integration
- Full role-based access with department-level permissions
- Escalation rules (auto-escalate if no human response in X hours)
- Dedicated support + SLA
- White-glove onboarding and connector setup
