# Founder Perks & Money Moves

Simple tax-efficient moves and perks applicable to any UK Ltd company you run.

---

## 1. Free Air Miles on Your Own Salary (Poodle)

**What:** Poodle payroll gives 4 Avios per £1 of salary processed. £2.50 per wage slip.

**Optimal setup (zero tax, free miles):**
- Pay yourself £1,047/month (NI threshold — no income tax, no NI)
- Use Poodle as payroll provider
- Cost: £2.50/month = £30/year
- Earn: ~50,000 Avios/year

**What 50k Avios gets you annually:**
- Return Dubai business class (BA ~50,000 off-peak) ✓
- Return New York economy (26k) + 2× European breaks (12k each) ✓
- Return Bangkok economy (~35k) + a city break ✓

**Monthly accumulation:**
| Months | Miles | What it covers |
|---|---|---|
| 3 | 12,564 | Return Europe economy |
| 6 | 25,128 | Halfway to NYC |
| 12 | 50,280 | Long-haul return or multiple short-haul |

**Rules:**
- Avios don't expire if account used once every 3 years
- Dividends don't earn miles — salary only
- Works for any Ltd company (JobFilter, Fitrah, anything)

## 2. Director Salary + Dividends Split

**Why:** Salary above £12,570/yr gets taxed at 20%. Dividends taxed at 8.75% (basic rate).

**Optimal for solo founder:**
- £12,570/yr salary (£1,047/mo) — zero income tax, zero NI
- Everything else as dividends — 8.75% tax vs 20%+ on salary
- Corporation tax on profits first (19–25%), then dividends from what's left

**Don't exceed:** £50,270 total income (salary + dividends) without going into higher rate (33.75% dividend tax).

---

## 3. Home Office Allowance

- Claim £6/week flat rate (HMRC simplified) — no receipts needed
- Or claim proportion of actual bills (broadband, heating, electricity) if working from home
- Works even if you rent — just proportion of rent isn't claimable, but bills are

---

## 4. Phone & Broadband Through the Company

- Put your phone contract through the Ltd company — fully deductible
- Broadband: claim business proportion (e.g. 80% if mainly for work)
- Saves you the VAT + reduces corporation tax bill

---

## 5. Mileage Claims (If You Drive)

- Claim 45p/mile for first 10,000 miles/year through your company
- This is tax-free to you and deductible for the company
- Keep a simple log: date, from, to, miles, purpose

---

## 6. Training & Software — Run Through the Company

- Any course, book, tool, or subscription related to your business = company expense
- Claude Pro, Cursor, Figma, Notion, domain names, hosting — all deductible
- Reduces corporation tax (saves 19–25p per £1 spent)

---

## 7. Annual Investment Allowance

- Buy equipment (laptop, desk, phone, camera) through the company
- 100% deductible against profit in the year of purchase
- A £1,500 laptop saves ~£285–375 in corporation tax

---

## 8. Pension Contributions (Most Underused)

- Company pension contributions are fully corporation tax deductible
- No NI on them either (unlike salary)
- Even £200/month into a SIPP = £2,400/yr not taxed
- At 25% corp tax rate, £2,400 contribution costs you only £1,800 net
