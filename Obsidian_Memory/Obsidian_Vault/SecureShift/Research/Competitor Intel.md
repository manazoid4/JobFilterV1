# SecureShift — Competitor Intel

## Direct Competitors

### SecurityJobsUK.co.uk
- **Model:** Pay-per-post (~£99/listing)
- **Weakness:** Dated UI, no SIA verification, no seeker profiles, no scraping
- **Traffic:** Low. Minimal SEO.
- **Opportunity:** Easy to outrank. Better UX wins instantly.

### GuardingJobs.com
- **Model:** Employer listings, agency-heavy
- **Weakness:** No SIA badge/verification, thin content
- **Opportunity:** SIA verification differentiator kills them.

### Indeed / Reed / Totaljobs
- **Model:** Generic boards
- **Weakness:** Not SIA-aware. No verification. Poor shift/temp UX.
- **Opportunity:** Beat on niche relevance. Scrape them for supply.

### Facebook Groups / WhatsApp
- **Model:** Informal market — where SIA workers actually find work today
- **Weakness:** No vetting, no accountability, no pay transparency, no searchability
- **Opportunity:** This is the real competitor. Replace it with a clean platform.
