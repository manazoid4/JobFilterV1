# SecureShift — Product Overview

## Problem
~400k SIA-licensed workers in UK. They find work via WhatsApp groups, word of mouth, generic boards (not SIA-optimised), or agencies that take 15–25% markup. No dedicated niche platform.

## Solution
UK-only job board for SIA-licensed security and temp hospitality.
- **Seekers:** Browse jobs, verify SIA, apply direct.
- **Employers:** Post roles, verify candidates, hire without agency fees.

## Differentiators
1. SIA verification built-in (public register check)
2. Scraper-populated — board is full day 1
3. Shift/temp UX — not just full-time job listings
4. Niche SEO dominance: "SIA jobs [city]" long-tail
5. Direct hire, flat fee — no agency cut

## Users

### Seeker (primary)
Door supervisor, CCTV operator, event/retail/hospitality security. 18–45, UK. Wants shifts, fair pay, easy apply. Pain: jobs scattered across WhatsApp/Facebook.

### Employer (secondary — monetization)
Small venues, event companies, national agencies. Wants vetted staff fast. Pain: agency markup, slow turnaround.

## MVP Scope
- [ ] Job board (scraped + employer-posted)
- [ ] Search + filter (role, location, SIA flag)
- [ ] Seeker profile + SIA badge
- [ ] Employer job posting
- [ ] Stripe subscriptions
- [ ] SIA licence verification
- [ ] Email job alerts
