# Employer Monetization — Unlock Sequence

## The Gate

**Do not sell to employers until 1,000 unique monthly visitors (seeker side).**

Employers pay for access to candidates — not promises. Prove the asset exists first, then pitch.

---

## Phase 1 — Build the Asset (0 → 1k/mo)

Seeker-only. Employers get nothing yet.

- Scrapers fill board with real jobs
- 120 SEO pages rank for "{role} jobs {city}"
- Free seeker signups (collect emails)
- Seeker Pro (£9.99/mo) = first revenue

**Before Phase 2, must hit all three:**
- [ ] 1,000 monthly visitors
- [ ] 200+ seeker profiles
- [ ] 50+ monthly applications (proves real intent)

---

## Phase 2 — Soft Employer Entry (1k → 5k/mo)

**Unlock:** 1 free employer job post/month. No paywall yet.

1. Allow free posting — require real company, location, pay rate, SIA toggle
2. Outreach to small venues in top 10 cities: "We have [X] verified SIA candidates in [city]. Post free."
3. Use Outreach Composer agent. Personalised, 150-word emails.
4. Target: 20 active employers posting free
5. Show them results: "Your listing got 14 applications in 3 days"
6. Then ask for £49/mo Starter.

---

## Phase 3 — Revenue Ladder (5k/mo+)

Work up every rung. Don't skip.

| Rung | Product | Price | Trigger to upgrade |
|------|---------|-------|--------------------|
| 1 | Free (1 post/mo) | £0 | — |
| 2 | Starter Monthly | £49/mo | Need >1 post/mo |
| 3 | Starter Annual | £399/yr | After 3mo monthly |
| 4 | Agency Pro Monthly | £149/mo | Need unlimited + ATS |
| 5 | Agency Pro Annual | £1,199/yr | Lock in, save £589 |
| 6 | Enterprise | £300+/mo | National, 50+ posts |

### Conversion triggers that actually work

- **Volume:** "30 applications on your free post this month"
- **Urgency:** "Upgrade before weekend — post 5 roles now"
- **Quality:** "3 applicants are SIA-verified, 4+ years experience"
- **FOMO:** "Agency X is featured above you. You're not."
- **Batch need:** "You have 12 Saturday shifts — CSV import is Starter+"

- Never offer free posting indefinitely
