# SecureShift — Pricing

## Philosophy
- Annual-first: toggle defaults to annual on pricing page
- Show £ saved in green, not % (more concrete)
- Free tier drives seeker volume → employer value prop
- Agency tier is primary ARR driver

## Seeker Plans

| Plan | Monthly | Annual | ARR/user |
|------|---------|--------|----------|
| Free | £0 | £0 | £0 |
| Pro | £9.99 | £79/yr | £95 |

**Pro:** Unlimited apps · Priority placement · Instant alerts · CV visible · SIA badge · DM employers

## Employer Plans

| Plan | Monthly | Annual | ARR/account |
|------|---------|--------|-------------|
| Starter | £49 | £399/yr | £479 |
| Agency Pro | £149 | £1,199/yr | £1,438 |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom | £3,000+ |

**Agency Pro:** Unlimited posts · Featured listings · ATS · 3 seats · CSV import · SIA batch verify

## Revenue Scenarios

| Mix | ARR |
|-----|-----|
| 100 Seeker Pro + 10 Agency | ~£30k |
| 200 Seeker Pro + 25 Agency | ~£69k |
| 500 Seeker Pro + 50 Agency | ~£149k |
