## Go-To-Market

**Phase 1 — Scraper First**
- Scrape Indeed, Reed, CV-Library, Totaljobs daily
- Populate board without employer sign-up
- SEO pages: "{role} jobs {city}" for long-tail Google

**Phase 2 — Seeker Traffic**
- Target: 1,000+ monthly visitors
- Free seeker accounts → pro upsell £9.99/mo

**Phase 3 — Employer Monetization**
- Proven candidate pool → pitch agencies
- Employer Starter £49/mo · Agency Pro £149/mo
