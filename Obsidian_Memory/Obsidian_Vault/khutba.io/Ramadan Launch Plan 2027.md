# Ramadan Launch Plan 2027

> Ramadan 2027 estimated: March 1, 2027. Launch window: January 2027.

## Why Ramadan Matters

Ramadan is the ONE time every masjid committee thinks about improving the congregation experience. Attendance goes up 3-5x. Non-Arabic speakers attend more. The need for translation is most visible.

**Revenue impact:** Ramadan acquisition could drive 30-40% of annual signups.
