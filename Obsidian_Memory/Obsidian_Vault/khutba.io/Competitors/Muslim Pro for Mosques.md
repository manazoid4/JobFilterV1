# Muslim Pro for Mosques — Potential Competitor Analysis

**URL:** https://www.muslimpro.com
**Type:** Potential future competitor — consumer prayer app exploring B2B

## What They Do
Muslim Pro is the world's most popular Muslim consumer app with 50M+ downloads. Features include:
- Prayer times and Qibla direction
- Quran reading and audio
- Islamic calendar and events
- Community features
- Halal restaurant finder
- Charity/donation features

## Current B2B Offering: ❌ None
Muslim Pro is currently a consumer-only product. They do NOT offer any B2B or mosque-focused tools.

## Why They're a Threat
- **50M+ user base** — massive distribution if they go B2B
- **Well-funded** — IslamicFinder/MP Labs has raised significant capital
- **Brand recognition** — every Muslim knows Muslim Pro
- **Technical capability** — they have the engineering resources to build anything
- **Data advantage** — they know where Muslims live, pray, and which masjids they attend

## What They Could Build
If Muslim Pro decides to build a B2B mosque product, they could:
1. Live khutbah translation (direct khutba.io competitor)
2. Mosque management platform (competing with Al-Mosque, Pillars)
3. Mosque discovery and check-in features
4. Donation processing for masjids
5. Community announcement platform

## Likelihood: 🟡 MEDIUM (18-36 month horizon)
- They have the resources but haven't shown B2B interest yet
- Their core business (consumer app + ads + subscriptions) is working
- B2B is a different sales motion than they're used to
- However, the mosque tech market is growing and they would notice

## khutba.io vs Muslim Pro (Hypothetical)

| Feature | Muslim Pro (consumer) | khutba.io |
|---|---|---|
| Target | Individual Muslims | Masjid committees |
| Revenue model | Consumer subscriptions + ads | B2B SaaS subscriptions |
| Live translation | ❌ Not built | ✅ Core product |
| Screen display | ❌ Not built | ✅ Core product |
| Mosque relationships | Indirect (through users) | Direct (selling to committees) |
| Sales motion | App store download | Direct sales + word-of-mouth |

## Key Takeaway
Muslim Pro is not a threat today. But if they decide to build a B2B mosque product, they could move fast and leverage their existing user base. khutba.io should establish deep relationships with UK masjids before Muslim Pro (or any large player) enters the space.

## Risk Level: 🟡 MEDIUM (long-term)
- No current B2B product
- But they have the resources, brand, and data to build one
- 18-36 month threat horizon

## Action
- Monitor Muslim Pro for any B2B announcements
- Build strong relationships with UK masjids now (switching costs increase over time)
- Focus on the screen-first niche — Muslim Pro is phone-first by nature
- Consider partnering with Muslim Pro if they go B2B (become their translation provider)
