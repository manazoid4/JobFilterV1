# UK Mosque Outreach Strategy

> Beachhead: Birmingham → UK national → English-speaking Europe.

## Why Birmingham First

- 150+ masjids in Birmingham alone
- Large South Asian Muslim population (Urdu, Bengali, Somali speakers)
- Multi-ethnic congregations = high need for translation
- Local trust matters — "built in Birmingham" is a real advantage
- Lower competition than London
- Easier to do in-person demos and support

## Outreach Channels

### 1. WhatsApp (Primary — 70% of effort)

**Why:** Masjid committees communicate on WhatsApp. Decisions are made there. Recommendations spread there.

**Tactics:**
- Create shareable WhatsApp messages (not emails)
- Build a WhatsApp bot for masjid admins to try khutba.io
- Encourage existing users to share in their committee WhatsApp groups
- Create short demo videos optimized for WhatsApp sharing (vertical, <60 seconds)
- Offer referral discounts ("Refer another masjid, get 1 month free")

**WhatsApp message template:**
> "Assalamu Alaikum. We started using khutba.io at [Masjid Name] — it shows live translations of the khutbah on our screen in Urdu, Bengali, and English. Takes 10 minutes to set up. Costs less than a box of flyers per month. Want me to show you? [link]"

### 2. In-Person Visits (20% of effort)

**Why:** Trust is built face-to-face. Especially with older committee members.

**Tactics:**
- Visit masjids after Jumu'ah (Friday prayer) — committee members are present
- Bring a laptop and demo khutba.io live
- Offer to set it up on the spot (10-minute setup)
- Leave printed one-pagers with QR code to trial

**Script:**
> "Assalamu Alaikum. I'm [name], I live in Birmingham. We built a tool that shows live translations of the khutbah on your screen — so everyone in the masjid can follow along, not just Arabic speakers. Can I show you? Takes 2 minutes."

### 3. Social Media (10% of effort)

**Why:** Supplementary reach. Younger committee members are on Facebook/Instagram.

**Tactics:**
- Facebook groups: "UK Muslims", "Birmingham Muslims", city-specific groups
- Instagram: Short demo videos, testimonials
- YouTube: 2-minute product demo
- TikTok: Not priority, but short demos could reach younger imams

## Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|---|---|
| "We already have a volunteer translator" | "Free the volunteer. Let them pray instead of translating. The screen does it automatically." |
| "Too expensive" | "Less than a box of flyers per month. £29/mo. Your congregation will notice the difference every Friday." |
| "Tech is unreliable" | "Works on any laptop + screen. If it works on YouTube, it works with khutba.io. Offline fallback if internet drops." |
| "We don't have a screen" | "Any TV, projector, or monitor works. Most masjids already have one for prayer times." |
| "Arabic displays wrong" | "RTL is built in from day one. Arabic, Urdu — both display correctly." |
| "We're happy with what we have" | "What do you currently use? [Listen]. khutba.io is purpose-built for khutbah translation — not a general tool." |
| "Let me ask the committee" | "Of course. Here's a WhatsApp message you can forward to them: [share template]. I'm also happy to join your next committee meeting." |
| "We'll think about it" | "No problem. Ramadan is coming — that's when the need is biggest. I'll check back in [timeframe]." |

## Referral Program

**Mechanics:**
- Existing masjid refers another masjid
- New masjid signs up and pays for first month
- Referring masjid gets 1 month free
- No limit on referrals

**WhatsApp referral template (auto-generated for each masjid):**
> "Assalamu Alaikum. [Masjid Name] uses khutba.io for live khutbah translations. We've been using it for [X] months — our congregation loves it. If you want to try it: https://khutba.io?ref=[masjid-id]"

**Tracking:**
- Unique referral link per masjid
- Automatic credit applied to referring masjid's account
- Monthly leaderboard (optional — some masjids like recognition)

## Partnerships

### Islamic Scholars / Imams
- Identify 3-5 respected UK imams
- Offer free khutba.io for their masjid
- Ask for testimonial / endorsement
- Their word carries weight with other masjids

### Islamic Organisations
- Muslim Council of Britain (MCB)
- local mosque forums
- Islamic Relief UK (potential funding partner)
- Muslim Aid (potential funding partner)

### Universities
- University Islamic societies (Birmingham, Manchester, Leeds)
- Offer free khutba.io for university masjid events
- Student ambassadors for outreach
