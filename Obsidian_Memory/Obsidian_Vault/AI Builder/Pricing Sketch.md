# Pricing Sketch

## Goal

Keep it simple and low-friction at entry, but leave room for meaningful paid usage.

## Suggested model

- Free: limited captures, limited model routing, no advanced exports
- Founder: cheaper early-access monthly price
- Standard: normal monthly price
- Annual: discounted yearly rate

## Simple structure

- Free
- Founder
- Standard

## Pricing direction

Use the same pattern as JobFilter:

- discounted founder tier
- clear standard monthly price
- annual discount
- no confusing feature maze

## Monetisation ideas

- extra capture packs
- model routing upgrades
- team/shared workspace
- premium prompt libraries
- export packs
- voice capture add-on

## Note

This should feel like a product people keep open, not a tool they only buy once.
