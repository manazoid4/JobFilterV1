# Profit Maxing Sheet

## Goal

Work out how many users are needed to make the product profitable, then map how long it takes to reach each user target.

## Core formula

Monthly profit =

`(free users x free value) + (founder users x founder ARPU) + (standard users x standard ARPU) + (annual cash-in / 12) - fixed costs - variable costs`

## Break-even formula

Break-even paid users =

`fixed monthly costs / gross margin per paid user`

## Growth timing formula

Months to target =

`target paying users / net new paying users per month`

## Example pricing shape

- Free
- Founder: lower monthly price for early users
- Standard: full monthly price
- Annual: discounted annual prepay

## Questions the sheet should answer

- How many paying users cover hosting, APIs, support, and growth?
- What founder count is enough to fund launch?
- At what user count does the business become self-funding?
- What monthly churn kills the model?
- How much prepay cash comes from annual plans?

## Assumptions to fill in

- churn rate by tier
- average time to first value

## Note

This sheet should be kept in sync with pricing, actual costs, and user growth, then used to decide when to raise prices or add more paid tiers.
