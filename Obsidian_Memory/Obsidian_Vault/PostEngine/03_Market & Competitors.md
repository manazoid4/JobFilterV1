# PostEngine — Market & Competitors

---

## Market Size

- UK small businesses: ~5.5 million
- Self-employed tradespeople: ~2 million
- Of those, most have zero or inconsistent social presence
- Even capturing 0.1% at £29/mo = £580k ARR

---

## Direct Competitors

### Metricool
- **Price:** Free → $18/mo → $45/mo
- **Strengths:** Generous free tier, solid analytics, Claude MCP integration, wide platform support
- **Weaknesses:** Built for everyone = speaks no one's language. No AI writing at the core. UI is cluttered. 40+ features most never use.
- **Attack:** "Metricool is a great tool if you know what to write. We write it for you."

### Buffer
- **Price:** Free → $6/channel/mo
- **Strengths:** Clean UX, reliable scheduling, good free tier (30 posts/channel/mo)
- **Weaknesses:** No AI writing. No niche templates. Feels generic.
- **Attack:** Same as Metricool — it's a scheduler, not a content engine.

### Later
- **Price:** Free → $18/mo
- **Strengths:** Instagram-first, visual calendar, good Reels support
- **Weaknesses:** Very Instagram-focused. No Reddit. No AI.
- **Attack:** Single-platform thinking. Limited.

### Hootsuite
- **Price:** $99/mo+
- **Strengths:** Enterprise features, team collaboration
- **Weaknesses:** Overpriced for solo users. Complex. Agency-first.
- **Attack:** Not for you. You don't have a team.

### Publer
- **Price:** Free → $12/mo
- **Strengths:** Reddit support, reasonable pricing
- **Weaknesses:** Reddit scheduler is weak. No AI writing.
- **Attack:** Reddit scheduling half-works. AI is an afterthought.

### Taplio / Supergrow
- **Price:** $39–$79/mo
- **Strengths:** LinkedIn-specific AI
- **Weaknesses:** LinkedIn only. Expensive.
- **Attack:** One platform for £60/mo. We do three for £29.

---

## The Gap

**No tool exists that:**
1. Writes the posts for you (genuinely, not a "rewrite" button)
2. Knows your specific trade / niche
3. Covers Instagram + LinkedIn + Reddit
4. Costs less than £30/mo

That's the gap.

---

## Niche Wedge Strategy

Start: UK tradesmen (builders, electricians, plumbers, roofers)
Then: Other UK small business verticals (hospitality, health/fitness, retail)
Then: Any small business owner globally

Each niche gets a tailored onboarding, pre-loaded post templates, and AI prompts tuned to their industry language. Metricool will never do this — they're too horizontal.
