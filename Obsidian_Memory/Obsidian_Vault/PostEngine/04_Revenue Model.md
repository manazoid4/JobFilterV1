# PostEngine — Revenue Model

---

## Pricing Tiers (v1)

| Tier | Price | What's included |
|------|-------|-----------------|
| **Free** | £0 | 10 posts/mo, Instagram only, basic AI, manual posting |
| **Solo** | £19/mo | 30 posts/mo, Instagram + LinkedIn, AI generation, auto-schedule |
| **Pro** | £39/mo | Unlimited posts, all 3 platforms (+ Reddit), analytics, priority AI |

**Founder price: £19/mo locked** for first 50 users (same tactic as JobFilter).

## MRR Targets

| Users | MRR |
|-------|-----|
| 10 | £190–£390 |
| 50 | £950–£1,950 |
| 100 | £1,900–£3,900 |
| 500 | £9,500–£19,500 |

**Goal:** £1,000 MRR before spending any money on marketing.

---

## Unit Economics

- Claude API cost per user: ~£0.50–£2/mo (50 posts × ~2p per generation)
- Supabase: Free up to 500MB / 50k users
- Vercel: Free up to reasonable usage
- Social APIs: All free
- **Gross margin at £19/mo: ~90%+**

---

## Revenue Gate Before Scaling

Do NOT spend on ads until:
- [ ] 50 paying users organically
- [ ] Churn under 10%/mo
- [ ] At least one niche has 20+ users posting consistently

---

## Growth Path

1. **Organic:** JobFilter user base + Reddit + LinkedIn + Instagram (same strategy as the product sells)
2. **Word of mouth:** Good product in a niche = tradesmen talk to other tradesmen
3. **Affiliate:** Pay £10 flat per referred paying user
4. **Agency upsell:** Once at 200+ solo users, add team/multi-brand tier at £79/mo
