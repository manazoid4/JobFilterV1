## Top 10 Findings

1. **Ghost leads are the #1 emotional trigger** for UK tradesmen. The anger is real, visceral, and under-addressed by every competitor.
2. **Checkatrade and MyBuilder are hated** for shared leads, annual fees, and low-quality enquiries. Tradesmen call them "Check-a-prat" and "MyTimeWaster."
3. **Free UK government data** (planning, EPC, Land Registry, Companies House) is legally usable under Open Government Licence and technically accessible.
4. **Tradesmen trust systems that save time**, not polished SaaS brands. Emotional honesty and tactical usefulness beat marketing gloss.
5. **Territory exclusivity** is a powerful moat. No competitor offers "one dominant partner per postcode cluster."
6. **The blueprint page reveals too much** about data sources and internal architecture. This is a competitive risk.
7. **Free tools are currently paywalled** via scan limits. This destroys trust — free means free.
8. **PatchLock terminology confuses** non-technical users. "Early Access" and "Territories" convert better.
9. **Mobile experience has hidden/cut-off sections** that need immediate responsive fixes.
10. **Field Notes and Trade Intelligence Briefings** can position JobFilter as an authority without requiring founder selfies or influencer branding.

## Top 10 Moat Opportunities

1. **Postcode exclusivity** — no competitor offers true territory locks.
2. **Official data fusion** — combining planning + EPC + Land Registry + Companies House into one score.
3. **Anti-ghost scoring** — behavioural + data signals that predict time-wasters.
4. **WhatsApp-first delivery** — tradesmen live on WhatsApp, not dashboards.
5. **Free tool ecosystem** — calculators and scorers that hook users before they pay.
6. **Trade Intelligence Briefings** — weekly tactical reports that make users smarter.
7. **Field Notes content** — operator-written insights that feel human, not AI-generated.
8. **Founder pricing lock** — £39/month forever creates switching cost and loyalty.
9. **Direct letters** — physical mail to high-value leads, something no SaaS competitor does.
10. **Speed to signal** — alerting within 24 hours of planning approval or property sale.
