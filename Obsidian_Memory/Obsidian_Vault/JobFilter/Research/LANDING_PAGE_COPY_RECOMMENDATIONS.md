# Landing Page Copy Recommendations

## Hero Section

### Current
"Stop quoting for tyre-kickers. Claim your trade territory before competitors do. JobFilter finds real construction signals, scores the money, and sends the best leads to WhatsApp."

### Recommended
"Stop quoting for ghosts."

Sub: "Real construction signals. Scored. Sent to your WhatsApp before anyone else knows the job exists."

CTA: "Scan my area — free" | "See how it works"

**Why:** Shorter. Punchier. "Ghosts" is more visceral than "tyre-kickers" and ties to existing brand language.

## How It Works

### Current
Three steps: Scan → Money Filter → Only money leads move.

### Recommended
Keep the three steps but add **micro-copy** under each:

1. **Scan your postcode**
   "Enter your working area. We check planning, EPC, land registry, and council contracts in real time."

2. **Money Filter ranks the job**
   "Urgency, value, distance, and buyer seriousness decide if it reaches your phone."

3. **Only gold leads move**
   "Weak signals stay out of your WhatsApp. You only see jobs worth chasing."

**Why:** Explains the "so what" for each step.

## Copy Rules (For All Pages)

1. **No startup jargon.** Never say "AI-powered", "machine learning", "SaaS", "platform".
2. **No passive voice.** "We send alerts" not "Alerts are sent."
3. **No vague promises.** "Real signals" not "better leads."
4. **Lead with pain.** "Quit working for ghosts" not "Find more jobs."
5. **End with action.** Every section needs a CTA.
6. **One idea per sentence.** No compound benefits.
7. **Use tradesman language.** "Quote", "site", "waste", "graft", "job".
