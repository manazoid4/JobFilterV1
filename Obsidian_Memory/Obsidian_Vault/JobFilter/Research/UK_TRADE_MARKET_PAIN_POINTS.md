## Pain Point 1: Ghost Leads

### Emotional Language
- "They only push low-value leads, and a lot of them are pretty pore (timewasters or people who don't even respond)"
- "Ghosted after I drove 40 minutes to quote"
- "Sent three quotes last week. Heard nothing."
- "Waste of diesel, waste of time, waste of my Saturday."

### Root Causes
1. Homeowners requesting quotes with no intention to buy.
2. Price shopping — collecting 5+ quotes to negotiate.
3. Change of mind after initial enquiry.
4. Platforms charging for "leads" that are just clicks.

### Financial Impact
- Average site visit cost: £25-50 (fuel + time).
- Average quote preparation: 1-2 hours.
- Tradesmen report 30-50% of enquiries are ghosted.
- Annual cost per tradesman: £2,000-5,000 in wasted quoting time.

### JobFilter Counter
**Ghost Risk Score**: Algorithmic assessment of buyer seriousness using planning data, property ownership, and signal timing. High-risk leads flagged before a visit is wasted.

## Pain Point 2: Shared Lead Marketplaces

### Emotional Language
- "Check-a-prat"
- "MyTimeWaster"
- "You pay them £1200 per year to give you leads... tell them to go do one!!"
- "By the time I called, two other builders had already quoted."

### Root Causes
1. Same lead sold to 3-5 tradesmen.
2. Race to the bottom on price.
3. Annual fees regardless of results.
4. Low barrier to entry = low-quality competition.

### Financial Impact
- Checkatrade: £600-1,200/year + lead costs.
- MyBuilder: 10-20% of job value.
- Rated People: £10-50 per lead.
- Bark: subscription + credit packs.

### JobFilter Counter
**Postcode Exclusivity**: One dominant partner per postcode cluster per trade. No shared leads. No bidding wars. The job is yours before anyone else knows it exists.

## Pain Point 3: Quote Fatigue

### Emotional Language
- "I'm sick of writing quotes for people who just want a number to beat."
- "Another tyre-kicker wanting a free estimate."
- "My missus says I spend more time quoting than working."

### Root Causes
1. No pre-qualification of enquiries.
2. No budget transparency.
3. Homeowners using quotes as negotiation ammo.
4. Time spent on unqualified leads.

### JobFilter Counter
**Serious Buyer Score**: Multi-source verification including planning status, property value, and recent activity. Only scored leads reach your WhatsApp.

## Key Takeaway

Tradesmen are not looking for more leads. They are looking for **better leads** and **less waste**.

JobFilter's entire positioning must revolve around **protection** and **time saving**, not volume.
