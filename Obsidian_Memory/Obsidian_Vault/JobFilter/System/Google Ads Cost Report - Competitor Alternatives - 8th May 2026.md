## RECOMMENDED ACTION PLAN

### Week 1-2: Setup
1. Create Google Ads account (if not exists)
2. Build 2 new comparison pages (`/vs/mybuilder`, `/vs/bark`)
3. Set up conversion tracking (waitlist form submit = conversion)
4. Create 3 ad groups: Competitor Alternatives, Generic Trade Leads, Trade-Specific

### Week 3-4: Stealth Test (£300 budget)
1. Launch with exact match only on 5 competitor alternative keywords
2. £10/day budget, UK-wide targeting
3. Monitor CTR, conversion rate, cost per sign-up
4. Kill keywords with CTR < 2% after 50 impressions

### Week 5-8: Scale Winners (£750/mo)
1. Increase budget to £25/day on converting keywords
2. Add phrase match variants
3. Build 2 more comparison pages if needed
4. A/B test ad copy: "Stop paying for shared leads" vs "Exclusive leads from £6.99/wk"

### Month 3+: Dominate (£1,500/mo)
1. £50/day budget across all keyword groups
2. Add Display retargeting for visitors who didn't convert
3. Expand to trade-specific keywords (plumbing leads, electrical leads, etc.)
4. Consider YouTube ads targeting trade-related content

## BOTTOM LINE

**Minimum viable test:** £300 over 30 days
**Break-even point:** 3 annual conversions or 10 monthly conversions
**Maximum recommended spend:** £1,500/mo (only after proving unit economics)

The "alternative" keyword space is white space — no competitor owns it. JobFilter can dominate these searches for less than £50/day while competitors spend £50,000+/month on generic terms.

The existing `/vs/buildalert` and `/vs/checkatrade` pages are already built and conversion-optimised. Two more comparison pages (`/vs/mybuilder`, `/vs/bark`) would unlock the highest-volume alternative keywords.
