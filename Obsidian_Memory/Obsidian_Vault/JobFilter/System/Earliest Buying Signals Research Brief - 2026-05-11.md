# Earliest Buying Signals Research Brief - 2026-05-11

## Goal

Find construction intent before a homeowner or business actively searches for a builder.

Most lead generation companies compete on:

- Google Ads
- SEO
- shared quote forms
- directories
- retargeting

JobFilter should identify intent before the customer asks for quotes.

## Required Output From Research Agents

For every signal, capture:

- data source
- legality and GDPR considerations
- update frequency
- automation difficulty
- estimated lead quality
- implementation cost
- competitive uniqueness
- exact implementation steps
- free or cheap APIs
- scraping possibilities
- automation architecture

## Signals To Research Deeply

### 1. EPC Changes And Energy Upgrades

People improving insulation, heat pumps, extensions, glazing, roofing, and solar often create downstream construction spend.

Research:

- EPC register access
- historical EPC comparison
- low EPC likely retrofit
- wealthy inefficient homes
- EPC plus planning plus property value fusion

### 2. Planning Permission Before Approval

Most competitors wait for approval.

Research:

- submitted applications
- pending applications
- neighbour consultation
- appeal stage
- withdrawn/revised applications

Goal: reach builders before competitors chase the same address.

### 3. Scaffold Intelligence

Research:

- scaffold company permits
- council road occupation permits
- temporary traffic orders
- skips
- pavement licences

If scaffolding appears, likely trades include roofing, rendering, extensions, solar, chimney work, and external repairs.

### 4. Skip Hire And Waste Signals

Research:

- council skip permits
- commercial waste permits
- construction disposal data
- environmental notices

A skip outside a house often means active spend.

### 5. Tradesman Exhaustion Signals

Find areas where builders are overloaded, slow to quote, or understaffed.

Potential data:

- poor marketplace response times
- quote-delay complaints
- local forum complaints
- review text
- postcode clusters with high demand and low supplier density

Sales angle:

> Builders in your postcode are overwhelmed. We found homeowners waiting for quotes.

### 6. Insurance Claim Signals

Research:

- storm damage
- flood claims
- roofing claims
- subsidence zones
- insurer tender panels
- weather-event construction spikes

Correlate weather, claim clusters, and repair demand.

### 7. Probate And Inheritance Property Signals

Inherited homes often need clearance, refurbishment, roofing, modernization, resale prep, and extensions.

Research:

- probate notices
- auction listings
- vacant homes
- Land Registry deltas
- dated ownership patterns

### 8. Commercial Fit-Out Signals

SME commercial work is underused by lead companies.

Research:

- licensing applications
- restaurant openings
- change-of-use applications
- retail permits
- HMO conversions
- Ofsted registrations
- clinic registrations

Likely trades: electricians, HVAC, fire safety, builders, signage, flooring.

### 9. Social Intent Extraction

Research low-cost methods for:

- Facebook local groups
- Reddit UK housing threads
- Nextdoor
- Instagram geo tags
- TikTok renovation videos
- "Can anyone recommend a builder?" posts

Do not build generic scraping. Use AI extraction for urgency, budget intent, ownership likelihood, and seriousness.

### 10. Satellite And Visual Change Detection

Advanced later-stage moat.

Research:

- free satellite imagery
- Google Earth historical imagery
- new roofs
- extensions
- solar installs
- groundwork
- skipped projects

## OSINT Topic

JobFilter is commercial construction intelligence.

Research how OSINT teams structure:

- enrichment pipelines
- entity resolution
- confidence scoring
- geospatial clustering
- anomaly detection
- source reliability
- analyst feedback loops

Apply legally and ethically to UK construction signals.
