## CRITICAL FINDING: JobFilter Has Direct Competitors Now

The previous vault (5 May) found no upstream competitors. This research found **five active UK services** pulling planning data for trades — all launched or scaled in 2025. **This changes the copy strategy.** JobFilter can no longer claim category uniqueness. It must claim superiority.

## What BuildAlert Does That JobFilter Does Not (Yet)

BuildAlert's letter-send model is clever and proven in direct mail. A physical letter to a homeowner who just got planning approval is a high-signal, low-competition action. Key difference from JobFilter:

- **BuildAlert:** Tradesman logs in → browses leads → clicks → BuildAlert prints and mails a physical letter. No WhatsApp. No scoring. No EPC. No contract data. No proactive push.
- **JobFilter:** System detects signal → scores it → pushes GOLD alerts via WhatsApp proactively. Tradesman never needs to log in to receive. Also covers EPC signals + council contracts, not just planning.

**Positioning response to BuildAlert:** "They send letters after you log in and find a lead. We WhatsApp you the lead before you know to look. And we don't charge per action — one price, unlimited alerts."

## What PlanLead Does That JobFilter Does Not (Yet)

PlanLead generates AI-written outreach letters. Same upstream data layer + an AI copywriting layer on top. No WhatsApp delivery. No EPC/contract signals. Price unknown.

**Positioning response to PlanLead:** "They write the letter for you. We WhatsApp you first. A well-written letter sent a day late still loses to whoever turned up first. Speed beats copy."

## Landing Page Patterns

### Pattern 1: "One Number Hero" — The Jobber / ServiceM8 model

**What it is:** A single large statistic or social-proof number dominates the hero. Sub-headline clarifies the outcome. Single CTA.

**Tested result (2026 study, 2,000 pages):** Leading with a big number as the H1 beat every other hero pattern by +6% to +29% conversion lift.

**Examples verified May 2026:**
- Jobber homepage headline: "From first quote to final payment, Jobber keeps your work, team, and revenue moving forward." CTA: "Start free trial — no credit card required."
- ServiceM8: "Trusted to manage over £18B in jobs" — the number IS the proof.
- Housecall Pro: "200K Pros across 50+ trades" — scale proof above the fold.

**How JobFilter should use this:**
- Hero stat: "20,000+ planning applications scanned this week. How many are near you?"
- Sub: "JobFilter scans official UK planning data, EPC ratings, and council contracts. When something matches your trade and postcode, we WhatsApp you before it hits any directory."
- CTA: "See what's live near you — free" (no card, 3 scans/week)
- Social proof line below CTA: "First 30 trades lock in at £29/month forever."

**Why it works for trades:** Outcome-driven. A big number makes the opportunity tangible before they scroll. Tradesmen want to know the scale of what they're missing, not feature lists.

---

### Pattern 2: "The Comparison Takedown" — The Housecall Pro / Varga model

**What it is:** An explicit side-by-side table or two-column visual naming the old way vs the new way. Competitors named directly. Problem comes first, solution second.

**Tested result (2026):** 76% of high-converting demo pages display customer logos or competitor contrast above the fold. Pages using "us vs them" framing convert at significantly higher rates when the audience has prior bad experience with the named alternative.

**Examples verified May 2026:**
- Housecall Pro vs Jobber comparison page: names Jobber explicitly, walks through every feature category.
- Varga Media homepage: "Outdated model vs Our model" table — every column names a Checkatrade pain point.
- BuildAlert blog: "BuildAlert vs Rated People vs Checkatrade" — names competitors in content to capture comparison-intent traffic. (JobFilter should do the same.)

**How JobFilter should use this on `HomePage.tsx`:**

```
THE OLD WAY                              JOBFILTER

Checkatrade: £50–150/mo                  £29/month. Locked forever.
Leads sold to 4–8 trades                 Every signal is yours. No auction.
You call, filter, chase                  We WhatsApp you. You decide.
A form someone filled in                 Official planning data, EPC, contracts.
No guarantee of anything                 30-day money-back. Walk if it doesn't work.

Other planning tools: log in,            We push to your WhatsApp before
browse, find, send a letter (£2/time)    you know to check. One price. Unlimited.
```

- Name Checkatrade, MyBuilder, Rated People explicitly — not "other platforms"
- The new "other planning tools" row addresses BuildAlert/PlanLead without naming them (too small to give airtime)

### Pattern 3: "Instant Demo / Frictionless Free Tier" — The ServiceM8 / Jobber model

**What it is:** Instead of a traditional funnel, the product demo IS the landing page. Value is delivered before email is captured. Conversion happens after the user has seen real results.

**Tested result (2026):**
- Single CTA pages convert at 13.5% vs 10.5% for multi-CTA pages
- Removing navigation from trial/demo entry pages increases conversion 15–25%
- Forms above 4 fields: each additional field loses 10–15% conversion
- "Start free trial" outperforms "Get started" when the trial is genuinely frictionless
- Video testimonials increase conversion 80% vs text testimonials

**The common pattern across Jobber, ServiceM8, Housecall Pro (all verified May 2026):**
1. No credit card on free tier
2. Free tier is genuinely useful but creates natural friction (job limit or feature limit)
3. The paid tier feels like removing a constraint, not adding a product
4. "No credit card required" stated prominently next to CTA

**How JobFilter should use this:**
- Free: 3 scans/week. See lead title + rough location. No source, no address, no contact signal.
- The gate creates desire: tradesman sees "3-bed extension near you — [upgrade to see address and planning ref]"
- The gate IS the CTA. No separate conversion page needed.
- Add "3 free scans this week — X remaining" counter prominently on the scan page
- First won job by a user → record a 45-second screen capture of the GOLD lead card → put it on the homepage. Beats any written testimonial by 80%.
