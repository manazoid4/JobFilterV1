# Instagram — @jobfilter.uk

**Handle:** @jobfilter.uk  
**Status:** 0 posts, 0 followers — starting from scratch  
**Goal:** Build trust with UK tradesmen. Get first 100 followers organically. Drive waitlist sign-ups.

---

## Strategy

**One rule:** Every post must either:
- Save a tradesman time or money, OR
- Make them feel understood

No "check out our product" posts. No SaaS fluff. Pure trade-first value.

**Format mix (weekly):**
- 2× bold static graphic (pain point / tip / stat)
- 1× carousel (step-by-step or comparison)
- 1× reel (talking head or text-on-screen, 15–30 sec)
- 1× meme or relatable moment

**Aesthetic:**
- Black background + JobFilter yellow text for big statements
- White background + black text for tips/carousels
- Big condensed headlines — no soft fonts, no rounded corners
- No stock photos. Real sites, real tools, real language.

**Hashtags (add to every post):**
`#UKTrades #BuildingWork #Electrician #Plumber #Roofer #Builder #TradesmanLife #ConstructionUK #GoodLeads #SelfEmployed`

## Logo Brief

**Style:** Bold. Brutalist. Instantly readable at 30px.

**Concept A — Primary (recommended):**
- Black square background
- "JF" in thick white condensed sans-serif (like Impact or Barlow Condensed Black)
- Thin yellow underline or yellow dot after "JF"
- Simple enough to work as Instagram profile icon

**Concept B — Wordmark:**
- "JOBFILTER" in all-caps condensed bold, black on white
- Yellow full-stop or filter icon at the end: "JOBFILTER▸"
- Works for Stories header and bio link

**Font direction:** Barlow Condensed Black / Anton / Bebas Neue  
**Colours:** #0A0A0A (black), #FFD700 (yellow), #FFFFFF (white)  
**Do NOT use:** Rounded fonts, gradients, drop shadows, blue

## Growth Tactics (0 → 100 followers)

1. **Follow 20 UK trade accounts per day** (builders, electricians, plumbers — NOT competitors)
2. **Comment genuinely** on trade content — add value, don't pitch
3. **Post Stories daily** — even just a tip or question ("What's your worst shared lead story?")
4. **Use location tags** — London, Manchester, Birmingham construction posts
5. **DM the first 50 followers** personally — thank them, ask what their biggest lead problem is
6. **Cross-post** the News page content from jobfilter.uk into Stories
