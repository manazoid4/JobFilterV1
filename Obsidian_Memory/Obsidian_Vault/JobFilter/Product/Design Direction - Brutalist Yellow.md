# Design Direction - Brutalist Yellow

## Keep
- White / black / JobFilter yellow.
- Thick borders, hard shadows, boxy buttons.
- Big condensed headlines.
- Simple trade-first navigation: What You Get, Testing Stage, Blueprint, Login, Get Started.
- Union flag mark next to JobFilter.
- Black panels for serious product sections.
- Yellow bands for strong offers and pricing.

## Polish
- Keep the general localhost look.
- Improve spacing, mobile fit, and hierarchy.
- Keep content short and direct.
- Do not soften the brand into a generic SaaS look.

## Avoid
- Dark navy hero.
- Rounded soft cards.
- Purple or blue gradients.
- Corporate copy.
- Changing the overall style without checking this note.
