# Launch Scenarios

Updated: 2 May 2026

## Core Pay Test

Question:

Can one strong lead per week justify payment?

Answer:

Yes, if the lead is local, recent, high intent, not flooded with competitors, and has a clear next action.

## Scenario 1: Small Trade Job

- Example: £800 job
- Gross margin: 35%
- Gross profit: £280
- One won job per week: about £1,120 gross profit per month
- Subscription fit: £49-£99/month is easy to justify

## Scenario 2: Mid-Size Domestic Job

- Example: £4,000 job
- Gross margin: 30%
- Gross profit: £1,200
- One won lead per month can justify £99-£199/month
- One strong lead per week makes JobFilter feel underpriced

## Scenario 3: Builder / Extension / Renovation

- Example: £15k-£40k job
- Gross margin: 15-25%
- One won job per quarter can justify £199-£497/month
- Quality has to be high. Weak shared leads will kill trust

## Scenario 4: Weak Shared Leads

- Vague scope
- No budget
- Late source
- Already being chased by several trades
- No urgency

Verdict:

BIN IT.

This is where JobFilter must be different from generic lead sites.
