# Competitive Intelligence: Belvoir Maintenance & FixFlo

## Date: 2026-05-11
## Source: Web research + market analysis

---

## Executive Summary

The UK rental property maintenance market is worth **£29.7 billion annually** across **10.6 million rental properties**. It is dominated by **reactive workflows** with no proactive detection. This is JobFilter's primary disruption opportunity.

---

## 1. BELVOIR MAINTENANCE

### What It Is
- One of the UK's largest property franchise networks: **150-170 offices**, **65,000+ properties**
- NOT a tech platform — traditional letting agent franchise with maintenance as a service add-on
- Most branches use FixFlo integration for maintenance workflow

### How It Works
1. Tenant reports issue (FixFlo portal, phone, email, or branch-specific portal)
2. Property manager triages during office hours (Mon–Fri 9am–5:30pm, Sat 9am–1pm, closed Sunday)
3. Landlord consultation for non-emergencies — can take "a couple of days"
4. Landlord decides: proceed, request quotes, decline, or self-allocate
5. Contractor dispatched from branch's "little black book"

### Critical Weaknesses
- **Completely reactive**: waits for tenant to report problems
- **Slow approval cycle**: landlord decision bottleneck adds days
- **Office hours only**: no weekend or evening triage
- **Limited trade choice**: branch-dependent contractor lists
- **No early signals**: misses planning applications, EPC gaps, permit activity

---

## 2. FIXFLO

### What It Is
- Repairs management software used by **40%+ of UK letting agents**
- Contractor Marketplace with auto-assignment "Co-pilot"
- **4,000+ contractor companies**, **70,000+ individual contractors**

### How It Works
1. Tenant reports issue through agent-branded FixFlo portal
2. System triages and suggests contractors based on trade, location, availability
3. Agent approves and assigns
4. Contractor receives job notification
5. Job completion tracked in dashboard

### Critical Weaknesses
- **Purely reactive**: no proactive detection whatsoever
- **Charges trades**: contractors pay to be on the platform
- **High competition**: 70,000+ contractors competing for same jobs
- **Race to the bottom**: shared leads create price pressure
- **No signal intelligence**: cannot detect work before tenant reports it

## 4. THE REACTIVE MAINTENANCE FLOW (Industry Standard)

```
Tenant reports problem
    ↓
Agent triages (office hours only)
    ↓
Landlord approves (1-3 days)
    ↓
Trade assigned from pool
    ↓
Trade quotes (competing with 3-5 others)
    ↓
Job completed
```

**Average time from report to assignment: 3-7 days**
**Average trades quoting per job: 3-5**
