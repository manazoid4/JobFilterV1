# Comparison Pages

Created: 8th May 2026

## Purpose
Capture comparison-intent search traffic and convert it. 76% of high-converting demo pages display competitor contrast above the fold.

## Pages

### `/vs/buildalert` — JobFilter vs BuildAlert
- **File:** `src/pages/CompareBuildAlertPage.tsx`
- **Target keywords:** "buildalert alternative", "buildalert vs", "planning leads comparison"
- **Angle:** Honest — BuildAlert is good at planning alerts. But planning is only 1 of 5 signals JobFilter scans.
- **Key differentiators:**
  - 5 signals vs 1 (planning only)
  - WhatsApp delivery vs physical mail (£2/letter)
  - GOLD/SILVER/BIN scoring vs raw data dump
  - £29/mo unlimited vs £2/letter (adds up fast)
  - EPC, contracts, land registry, companies house — none covered by BuildAlert
- **CTA:** "Try JobFilter free — see what BuildAlert doesn't show you"

### `/vs/checkatrade` — JobFilter vs Checkatrade
- **File:** `src/pages/CompareCheckatradePage.tsx`
- **Target keywords:** "checkatrade alternative for tradesmen", "checkatrade too expensive"
- **Angle:** Focus on pain trades already feel — shared leads, price wars, monthly fees just to be listed
- **Key differentiators:**
  - Exclusive leads vs shared (sold to 4-8 trades)
  - £29/mo all-in vs £50-90/mo per category + per-lead fees
  - Official signals (planning, EPC, contracts) vs homeowner form submissions
  - Scoring (GOLD/SILVER/BIN) vs all leads look the same
  - No contracts vs 12-month memberships reported
  - 30-day guarantee vs no refund policy
- **CTA:** "Stop competing. Start filtering."

## Design Rules
- Brutalist yellow/black/white — matches main site
- Honest, not mean — trades respect straight talk
- Every claim must be defensible (sourced from competitor intel doc)
- Mobile-first — comparison tables scroll horizontally on small screens
- Internal links to `/pricing`, `/find-jobs`, `/signals` on every page

## SEO Notes
- BuildAlert page targets planning-data comparison searchers (higher intent, closer to purchase)
- Checkatrade page targets frustrated directory users (higher volume, more emotional)
- Both pages should be linked from homepage "vs" section once traffic data confirms performance
- Consider adding schema markup for comparison tables once pages are live

## Content Principles
- Never use: "AI-powered", "leverage", "optimise", "dashboard", "synergy"
- Always use: outcome-first copy, weekly pricing framing, competitor count urgency
- Headlines: short declarative statements
- Sub-copy: one sentence max. Prove the claim. Move on.
- No mascots, no emojis in product copy, no exclamation marks
