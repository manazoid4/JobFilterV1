# FixFlo Competitor Research & Product Inspiration

## Date: 2026-05-11
## Market: Property Maintenance Management (UK/EU/Global)

---

## Executive Summary

The property maintenance management market is rapidly evolving with AI-driven disruption. FixFlo dominates UK letting agents (~40% share, 70,000+ contractors, 1.6M+ units) but faces growing pressure from AI-native entrants.

**UK market size**: £281M (2025) → £537M by 2033 (8.5% CAGR)
**Global market**: $15.8B (2024) → growing at 9.2% CAGR

## Key Market Trends

### 1. AI-Native Triage
- 2025-2026 is the shift from "digital intake" to "AI agent triage"
- Lanten, askporter, Fixzy all use AI to handle initial tenant reports
- Cost reduction: AI triage cuts admin time by 60-80%

### 2. Proactive Maintenance
- IoT sensors (Glu, iOpt, Airebo) detect issues before tenant reports
- Predictive algorithms flag high-risk properties
- Market moving from "fix when broken" to "prevent before failure"

### 3. Contractor Marketplace Evolution
- FixFlo's 70,000 contractors create race-to-bottom pricing
- New models: vetted elite networks (PropMaintain), skill-based matching (AXIO)
- Trend toward quality over quantity

### 4. Compliance Automation
- EPC C deadline (2028), gas safety, HMO licensing
- Run extend, Blockworx automate certificate tracking
- Growing regulatory burden = software opportunity

### 5. WhatsApp/Chat-First Interfaces
- Tenant preference: 78% prefer messaging over phone/apps
- Lanten, Fixzy use WhatsApp as primary interface
- Reduces friction vs downloading another app

## FixFlo's Specific Weaknesses (Opportunities)

From Trustpilot and user reviews:
1. **Terrible tenant UX**: Confusing interface, unclear status updates
2. **Contractor frustration**: Race to quote, low win rates, platform fees
3. **Agent pain**: Constant price increases, auto-renewal traps
4. **No proactive features**: Purely reactive, no predictive capability
5. **Slow support**: No phone support, email-only, slow responses
6. **Outdated UI**: Looks like 2015 software vs modern AI-native tools

## Product Inspiration: What to Build

### Option A: AI-Native Maintenance Platform
- **Core**: AI agent handles tenant reports via WhatsApp/SMS
- **Triage**: Automatic urgency classification, photo analysis
- **Dispatch**: Smart contractor matching (not just broadcast)
- **Proactive**: Integrate JobFilter signals (planning, EPC, permits)
- **Pricing**: £0.50/property/month (undercut FixFlo)

### Option B: Proactive Maintenance Intelligence
- **Core**: Detect maintenance needs before tenant reports them
- **Signals**: EPC gaps, planning applications, weather damage, boiler age
- **Delivery**: Alert landlord + suggest vetted trade
- **Moat**: JobFilter signal layer + landlord data
- **Pricing**: £29/month per landlord (unlimited properties)

### Option C: Trade-First Marketplace
- **Core**: Reverse the model — trades get exclusive leads, not shared
- **Signals**: JobFilter detects work, routes to 1 trade (not 70,000)
- **Value**: Higher win rates, better margins for trades
- **Pricing**: Trade pays £99/month for exclusive patch access
