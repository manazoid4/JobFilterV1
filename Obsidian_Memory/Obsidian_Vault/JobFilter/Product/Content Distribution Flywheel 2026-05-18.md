# Content Distribution Flywheel

Date: 2026-05-18

## Goal

Use JobFilter content to generate massive traction, users, signups, and MRR from UK tradesmen before paid acquisition.

## Core Strategy

JobFilter should publish practical trade intelligence that can be reused across:

- Instagram
- Reddit
- Quora
- Facebook groups
- LinkedIn
- TikTok
- Medium

The website should act as the source of truth. Social posts should point back to pages that convert: News, Find Jobs, free tools, pricing, waitlist, and trade-specific pages.

Every content asset must have a conversion job:

- start a free scan
- join the waitlist
- follow Instagram/TikTok/LinkedIn for retargeting
- claim a patch
- view pricing
- share/comment/reply with a real trade pain

## Content Pillars

1. Dead lead avoidance
2. Pricing discipline
3. Planning and EPC signals
4. Patch/territory strategy
5. Trade business operations
6. Quote and follow-up scripts
7. Weekly UK construction signal commentary

## Website Changes

- Add a landing-page content CTA with Instagram and News links.
- Expose News in navigation.
- Turn News into a reusable content hub with platform-specific repurposing prompts.
- Keep copy practical and trade-first. Avoid gimmicky language.

## Execution Rule

Every article should produce:

- A short site article
- Instagram carousel bullets
- TikTok/Reels talking points
- LinkedIn post
- Reddit/Facebook group discussion angle
- Quora answer angle
- Medium long-form angle

## Success Metric

Not page count. Not vanity views. Success is:

- profile clicks
- free scans started
- waitlist/signups
- pricing visits
- patch claim clicks
- paid checkout starts
- MRR
- returning visitors
- replies/comments from actual tradesmen

## MRR Loop

1. Publish useful trade content.
2. Push it to Instagram, Reddit, Quora, Facebook groups, LinkedIn, TikTok, and Medium.
3. Send traffic to News and free scan pages.
4. Capture email/phone/trade/postcode.
5. Retarget with patch scarcity and proof.
6. Convert to Founding 30 or Pro.
