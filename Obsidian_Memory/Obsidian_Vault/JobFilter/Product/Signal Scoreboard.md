## Highest Priority

1. Planning before approval
2. EPC F/G retrofit clusters
3. Scaffold and skip/site-activity signals
4. Commercial fit-out signals
5. Signal fusion around UPRN

## Why

Planning and EPC are fastest to automate from official data. Scaffold/skip/site-activity signals are more fragmented, but they produce very actionable evidence that work is already moving. The moat is not one source; it is the combined stack.
