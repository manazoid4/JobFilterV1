## Time To Scale UK (Deep-Dive 5 May 2026)

Source: https://timetoscale.co.uk/

**What they are:** Done-for-you marketing agency for UK trades. Builds websites, SEO, and automation. NOT a SaaS. NOT a lead marketplace. Human-powered, not software.

**Pricing:** Core Site £197/mo → TradeSite Growth £297/mo (most popular, 7/10 clients) → Market Leader £497/mo. Add-ons: SEO +£250/mo, PPC +£300/mo. 90-day minimum contract.

**Key features:** Functional Website (20-60 pages, 7-14 day build), Missed Call Text Back (auto-SMS <30s), Automated Lead Follow-Up (multi-touch SMS + email over 14 days), 5-Star Review Scaler (auto-request + sentiment routing), All-In-One Inbox (unified SMS/email/social), One-Click Campaigns (fill quiet weeks).

**Best copy:** "We build the engine, you bring the effort." / "Growth is a team sport." / "7x more likely to convert if contacted within 60 seconds."

**JobFilter advantage over them:**
1. Software (£29-49/mo) vs agency (£197-497/mo) — 10x cheaper
2. Finds leads proactively (upstream signals) vs captures leads reactively (website forms)
3. WhatsApp (98% open rate) vs SMS + email (20% open rate)
4. No minimum contract vs 90-day lock-in
5. Zero marginal cost to scale vs human-dependent

**What to emulate (updated):**
- Missed Call Text Back → Chase Engine: WhatsApp auto-nudge if lead not contacted within 2 hours
- Automated Lead Follow-Up → Chase Engine: draft message templates, multi-touch reminders
- 5-Star Review Scaler → Win Engine: auto-generate review link after Won job
- One-Click Campaigns → "Fill My Week" button: aggressive scan for 5+ jobs on demand
- Their outcome-first copy: "We find the work. You do the work. The system handles the rest."
- ROI framing: "One decent job covers your subscription for the year."
- Honesty positioning: "If you see nothing worth chasing, walk. No contract."

**What to avoid (unchanged):** Website/SEO agency territory. Stay upstream, stay software.

## Varga Media (Deep-Dive 5 May 2026)

Source: https://varga-media.com/

**What they are:** Hyper-lean paid ads agency for UK home improvement (4 trades only). Facebook/Instagram ads → human callers filter → deliver "qualified quotes." Single-page GoHighLevel site. Founded 2023.

**Pricing:** £1,000–£2,500/mo management + ad spend (total client cost £1,500–£5,500+/mo). "Get Quotes Or Don't Pay" guarantee.

**Trades:** Garden Rooms, Windows & Doors, Garage Conversions, Bathrooms.

**Best copy:** "Don't buy shared, time-waster leads. We fill your diary with high-quality, qualified & exclusive quotes that close, every single day." / "Get Quotes Or Don't Pay!" / Side-by-side "Outdated Model vs Our Model" table naming Checkatrade and Bark directly.

**JobFilter advantage over them:**
1. Software (£29-49/mo) vs agency + ads (£1,500–£5,500+/mo) — 50-100x cheaper
2. Official data sources (planning, contracts, EPC) vs paid ads (expensive, fatigues)
3. 8+ trades vs 4 trades
4. Product demo as qualification (free scan shows real results) vs quiz + sales call
5. Transparent pricing on-site vs "book a call to learn pricing"
6. Software scales infinitely vs human callers hit ceiling

**What to emulate:**
- "Old Way vs JobFilter" comparison table on homepage — directly name Checkatrade, MyBuilder, Rated People
- Money-back guarantee in hero copy — "30-day money-back. No quibbles."
- Risk reversal as primary trust signal — "Results or your money back"
- Naming competitors builds trust — tradesmen already know the enemies

**What to avoid:**
- Don't build a quiz funnel — the free scan IS the qualification
- Don't hide pricing behind a sales call — transparency wins
- Don't build on GoHighLevel templates — custom design is a moat
- Don't claim client numbers without verifiable proof

## GoHighLevel (Agency Platform — Competitor to TradieStack, not JobFilter)

Source: https://www.gohighlevel.com/

**What they are:** $97-497/mo all-in-one agency platform. CRM, websites/funnels, automations, SMS/email, pipelines, reputation management, invoicing, courses, communities. 60+ features. Used by agencies like Varga Media and Time To Scale to serve tradesmen at markup.

**Pricing:** Starter $97/mo (3 sub-accounts) → Unlimited $297/mo → Agency Pro $497/mo (SaaS mode, white-label). Add-ons: AI Employee +$97/mo, WhatsApp +$10/mo, White Label App +$497/mo.

**Why it matters:** Every competitor agency (Varga, Time To Scale, and dozens of others) runs on GoHighLevel. They pay $97-497/mo and charge tradesmen £1,000-£5,500+/mo. The gap between what the software costs and what tradesmen pay is the opportunity.

**TradieStack's advantage:** Skip the agency layer. Build the 18 features tradesmen actually use (not the 60+ they don't). Charge £29-49/mo directly. WhatsApp-native. JobFilter lead signals integrated. No sub-account agency complexity.
