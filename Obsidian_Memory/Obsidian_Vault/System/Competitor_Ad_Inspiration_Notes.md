# Competitor Ad Inspiration Notes

Created: 2026-05-18

---

## Competitor Pattern

### Bark.com

- **Promise:** "We do the legwork, so you don't have to." Connects customers with reviewed local professionals.
- **CTA:** Get quotes / find professionals
- **Pricing:** Free to list; leads charged per connection
- **Strongest angle:** Simple promise, local professionals, reviews
- **Weakness:** Shared leads — same request goes to multiple trades
- **JobFilter build implication:** Sharper trade-specific version: "JobFilter does the filtering before the rubbish reaches your phone." Admin Guard positioning: show JobFilter as holistic command centre, not just lead marketplace.

---

### LeadAdvisor

- **Promise:** High-quality leads for service businesses. Prospects book straight into your calendar.
- **CTA:** Start getting leads
- **Pricing/offer:** £99/month, cancel anytime, no setup fee
- **Strongest angle:** Calendar/follow-up integration language converts. Clear monthly value. Simple offer.
- **Weakness:** Generic — not trade-specific
- **JobFilter build implication:** Calendar export and follow-up language is powerful. Admin Guard must include calendar download and connect visually to lead follow-ups. Monthly pricing with simple cancellation is the winning offer model.

---

### GetBuilder

- **Promise:** Orange construction visual style. Simple comparison table. "£25 only if hired."
- **CTA:** Get matched / sign up
- **Pricing/offer:** Pay-per-hire model
- **Strongest angle:** Comparison tables are powerful. Strong contrast vs old lead-gen model. Proof of work required.
- **Weakness:** Only makes sense for larger jobs; small repeat tradespeople less well served
- **JobFilter build implication:** Comparison table built into Admin Guard teaser: "JobFilter Admin Guard vs Doing It Manually." Hard-contrast comparison drives signup intent.

---

### MTD Flow

- **Promise:** "MTD is coming." A tax app built for the trade.
- **CTA:** Join waitlist / find out more
- **Pricing/offer:** Not stated
- **Strongest angle:** MTD urgency is real. Construction visual style (orange/under construction). Early positioning on a real HMRC deadline.
- **Weakness:** Positioned as tax software — risks being seen as accountant replacement
- **JobFilter build implication:** MTD urgency is useful but must be safe. Admin Guard MTD Check card shows relevant date based on income band. Always defers to GOV.UK and accountant. NOT positioned as tax software.

---

### LeadToWork

- **Promise:** "You tile. We chase the leads." Auto lead scoring. Auto follow-up. App inbox visual.
- **CTA:** Free trial
- **Pricing/offer:** Free trial, subscription model
- **Strongest angle:** "You [trade]. We [do the hard part]" framing. Overlaps strongly with JobFilter.
- **Weakness:** Generic lead-chasing — not as deep on quality filtering or admin
- **JobFilter build implication:** JobFilter should lean harder into: lead filtering + scoring + follow-up prompts + admin reminders + Trade Command Centre positioning. Use: "You build. JobFilter filters, scores and reminds."

---

### The Trade Pilot

- **Promise:** "400+ UK tradespeople already on the waitlist." Jobs, invoices, certificates and HMRC tax return. All-in-one trade operating system.
- **CTA:** Join waitlist
- **Pricing/offer:** Not stated (waitlist/pre-launch)
- **Strongest angle:** Social proof (if real). All-in-one trade OS angle. Covers jobs + invoices + certs + HMRC.
- **Weakness:** "All-in-one" risks bloat. Do not fake numbers. Not launched yet = unproven.
- **JobFilter build implication:** Show locked future Admin Guard modules (Insurance, MOT, Gas Safe, VAT) to signal ambition without over-building. Do not fake social proof. Lean into "not another bloated trade app" — stay focused. JobFilter's differentiator is lead quality + filtering + scoring, not invoicing.

| Pattern | Implication |
