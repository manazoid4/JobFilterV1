# Health Brand Project — Overview

Created: 2026-05-18  
Status: Research + Strategy Phase  
Vault: Separate from JobFilter

---

## What This Is

A real health/performance brand built around ancestral foods, mineral hydration and masculine self-improvement.

Not a supplement brand.  
Not a fake testosterone brand.  
Not soft wellness.

A brand for men who train, eat real food, live with discipline and want a drink that is actually worth taking.

---

## First Product

**Red Mineral Tonic** — pomegranate + beetroot + electrolytes.  
Simple. Real. Masculine. Halal-conscious.

---

## Brand Foundation

- **Mission:** Real nourishment for men rebuilding strength, discipline and energy.
- **Enemy:** Ultra-processed fake supplements, synthetic pre-workouts, seed oil food, neon gym drinks.
- **Promise:** You don't need chemicals. You need minerals, roots and discipline.
- **Positioning:** Ancestral. Mineral-rich. Made for men who do the work.

## Ground Rules

- No fake health claims
- No medical advice
- No fake scarcity
- No expensive manufacturing too early
- Validate before spending
- Digital products first, physical second
- Halal ingredients only
- No alcohol-based extracts

---

## Phase Plan

1. **Now:** Name + formula + compliance + brand identity
2. **Week 1–2:** Free diagnostic tools + landing page + waitlist
3. **Week 3–4:** Founder offer + taste tests + digital product
4. **Month 2:** Pre-orders + first batch decision
5. **Month 3+:** Physical product if validated
