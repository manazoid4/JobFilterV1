# Content Strategy

---

## Core Principle

Content is not marketing. Content is proof.

Every post, video or caption should prove one of these:
- You know what you're talking about
- The product is real and getting better
- Real people like it
- You're building something honest

---

## Primary Platform: Instagram + TikTok

Start with one. Post the same content on both.  
Instagram = discovery + founder community  
TikTok = reach + new audience fast  

Choose the one you're more comfortable filming on. Then repurpose.

---

## Content Pillars (5)

### 1. Founder Journey
"I'm building this in real time. Here's what's happening."

- First taste test
- Formula changes
- Feedback sessions
- First batch production
- Honest results (good and bad)

**Why it works:** People back founders they trust. Showing the real journey builds more trust than any ad.

---

### 2. Ingredient Education
"Here's what's actually in the drink — and why it matters."

- Pomegranate: what polyphenols are, why antioxidants matter for training recovery, WITHOUT medical claims
- Beetroot: nitrate content, why athletes use it, blood flow without clinical claim language
- Sea salt: sodium vs. table salt, why electrolytes matter for hydration
- Lemon: vitamin C, alkalising effect, taste balance
- Coconut water: natural electrolytes vs. synthetic sports drinks

**Rules:** Never say "this cures X" or "clinically proven." Say "used by athletes," "traditionally consumed," "contains X which is a source of Y."

---

### 3. Food Philosophy
"Why real food beats supplements. Always."

- Butter vs. seed oils
- Raw milk philosophy (content only, not product)
- Tallow for cooking
- Eggs as the original protein
- Beef as ancestral food
- Ghee in cooking
- Why ultra-processed food is the enemy
- Halal food that is also genuinely good for you

**Why it works:** Positions Fitrah in a wider food philosophy movement. Builds audience beyond just the drink.

---

### 4. Masculine Health / Discipline
"Strength is built through consistency, not supplements."

- Morning routine content
- Training notes (no gym influencer aesthetics)
- Sleep and recovery — mineral connection
- Cold/hot exposure, sunlight, real food — no pseudoscience
- Discipline vs. motivation messaging
- Faith and discipline — for the Muslim audience, non-preachy for others

**Rules:** No cringe alpha content. No fake toughness. No bodybuilder aesthetics. Direct, grounded, real.

---

### 5. Behind-the-Scenes / Social Proof
"Here's what's actually happening."

- Taste tests (real reactions)
- Batch production
- Community feedback
- Founder answers questions
- Product evolution (bottle 1 vs bottle 5)
- First orders shipping

**Why it works:** Proof converts better than any copy.

## Content Format Mix

| Format | Frequency | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Short video (30–60s) | 3–4x per week | Reach + explanation |
| Photo + caption | 2–3x per week | Trust + education |
| Story (behind-scenes) | Daily when possible | Relationship |
| Long-form video (3–10 min) | 1–2x per month | Deep dive, YouTube |
| Email (to list) | 1–2x per week | Conversion |

---

## Caption Formula (for posts)

**Line 1:** Strong hook — no preamble. Make them stop scrolling.  
**Lines 2–5:** Short education, story or insight.  
**Line 6:** Clear CTA — "Quiz link in bio" / "Join founder list" / "Try this at home."

No long paragraphs. No emojis unless brand-appropriate (rare). Short lines.

---

## Topics — Idea Bank

**Easy wins (make these first):**
- "I stopped using pre-workout 14 days ago. Here's what I switched to."
- "What goes into the Rumman Red Tonic" (ingredient reveal)
- "The drink that replaced my energy drink habit"
- "Why I added sea salt to everything"
- "What pomegranate actually does for your training"
- "Halal and clean — why it's not easy to find a drink that is both"
- "I made 50 bottles and gave them away. Here's the honest feedback."

**Education content:**
- "Why your body needs sodium (not to be scared of it)"
- "What beetroot juice actually contains"
- "The difference between pomegranate juice and pomegranate extract"
- "Real food vs supplement: the honest comparison"
- "Why coconut water works as a base for electrolyte drinks"

**Philosophy content:**
- "Your grandfather ate butter. He didn't worry about cholesterol."
- "Raw milk: why I drink it and why I can't sell it"
- "The ultra-processed food problem nobody talks about"
- "Food that works vs food that just looks healthy"
- "Discipline is a practice not a product"

---

## What NOT to Post

- Before/after body transformation claims
- Medical comparisons ("this is better than X medication")
- Fake testimonials
- Staged reactions
- Anything that implies the drink treats a health condition
- Cringe alpha male content
- "Grind" culture content without substance
