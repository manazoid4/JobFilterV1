# Money-Making Strategy

---

## Core Model: Free → Trust → Pay

The same principle that makes JobFilter work, applied to health.

No cold sales.  
No expensive ads before validation.  
Free tools build trust → trust drives email list → list drives purchases.

---

## Free Diagnostic Tools

Build these to capture emails and qualify buyers.

### 1. Mineral Deficiency Risk Checker
"Answer 8 questions. See how mineral-depleted you might be."
Questions: water intake, energy levels, training frequency, sleep quality, cramp frequency, sweating, processed food intake, stress.
Output: Score + personalised recommendation → CTA to download guide or join waitlist.

### 2. Hydration Quality Score
"Is your current drink actually hydrating you — or just wetting your mouth?"
Questions about what they currently drink, how much, when.
Output: Score + "what your score means" + Fitrah position as the solution.

### 3. Training Fuel Checker
"Are you under-fuelled for the training you're doing?"
Questions about pre/post workout nutrition, energy during training, recovery.
Output: "You're leaving performance on the table" → CTA to get the free Mineral Hydration Guide.

### 4. "Are You Under-Fuelled?" Quiz
Short and punchy. 5 questions. Fast result.
Hooks the content audience who engage with short-form video.
Output: Badge (Under-fuelled / Adequately fuelled / Optimally fuelled) + next step CTA.

### 5. Testosterone Lifestyle Score (No Medical Claims)
"Not a medical test. Just a lifestyle check."
Questions: sleep hours, processed food frequency, training days per week, stress levels, sunlight exposure, real food intake.
Output: Lifestyle score + suggestions → email capture for full protocol guide.

## Founder Offer — "Founder 50"

Before spending money on manufacturing, sell the idea.

**What:**
- First 50 people to commit to the brand
- Locked-in founder price (20–30% below future retail)
- First batch guaranteed
- Private WhatsApp group with founder
- Behind-the-scenes formulation access
- Recipe drops during testing
- Lifetime "Founder" badge / recognition on packaging

**Price:** £49 one-time founder membership OR £29 first-batch pre-order with founder status.

**Conversion mechanism:**
- Waitlist quiz → emails → Founder 50 page
- No public countdown timer unless genuinely closing
- Real number counter only (don't fake it)
- Screenshot updates on social showing real signups

## Physical Product Plan (Month 2+)

### Phase 1: Small-batch local
- Make in registered shared kitchen
- Sell at local gym / market / mosque community / direct delivery
- 20–50 bottles per batch
- Focus on feedback and repeat buyers, not volume

### Phase 2: Pre-orders + co-packer
- Use founder pre-orders to fund first co-packed batch
- Minimum order quantities for co-packing: 500–2,000 units typically
- Cost per unit at scale: £1–£2.50 depending on ingredients and format
- Retail price: £3–£5 per bottle
- Target margin: 50–65% gross at scale

### Phase 3: Subscription
- Monthly box (12 bottles) at £30–£40/month
- Electrolyte sachet subscription
- Digital membership bundled

## Scarcity — Real Only

- First 50 founder memberships (actual hard cap)
- First batch (genuinely limited by kitchen capacity)
- First 20 Founder Challenge spots (limited by your time for 1:1)

Do not fake countdown timers.  
Do not fake "only 3 left."  
Real scarcity from real constraints builds more trust than manufactured urgency.
