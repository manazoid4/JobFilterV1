## Month 1 Exit Target

Before spending on manufacturing, hit these milestones:

- [ ] Formula scores 7+/10 avg from 10 real taste testers
- [ ] 200+ email list subscribers
- [ ] 20+ Founder 50 expressions of interest (not necessarily paid yet)
- [ ] 10+ digital product sales (£9 guide or £19 protocol)
- [ ] Food business registered
- [ ] Level 2 Food Hygiene Certificate completed
- [ ] At least 30 Instagram/TikTok posts published
- [ ] Zero money spent on manufacturing, ads, or design

If all milestones hit → proceed to Month 2: co-packer research, first physical batch, subscription launch.

## What to Deliberately NOT Do Yet

- Do not buy bottles, labels or packaging
- Do not commission a professional logo
- Do not run paid ads
- Do not approach retailers
- Do not get an investor
- Do not build a complex website
- Do not add any ingredient that needs Novel Food authorisation
- Do not sell raw milk
- Do not make health claims of any kind
- Do not fake reviews, scarcity, or numbers

## Decision Gate — End of Month 1

**Question to answer:** Do real people like this drink enough to pay for it?

If yes: Move to co-packer research, subscription model, physical launch.  
If no: Reformulate. Or pivot to selling digital products only while the physical product improves.

The digital products (guide, protocol) can generate cash regardless. The physical product needs proof first.
