## Session 1 — 2026-05-18

### What was decided

**Brand name:** Fitrah (return to natural state — Islamic concept, universally resonant)  
**Product name:** Rumman (pomegranate in Arabic — first product, story-forward)  
**Full product name:** Fitrah | Rumman Red Tonic

**First product:** Daily Mineral Hydration (Route 1)  
- Pomegranate juice + beetroot juice + coconut water + lemon + sea salt  
- Most achievable MVP. Lowest compliance risk. Clearest customer problem.

**Visual direction:** Black + Pomegranate Red + Warm Gold (Direction 3)  
- Strong, clean, masculine, no cringe  
- Affordable from day one  
- Works across all 3 audience types

**Money model:**  
1. Free quiz → email capture  
2. Digital products first (£9–£49)  
3. Founder 50 → pre-orders  
4. Small batch physical product (Month 2)  
5. Subscription (Month 3+)

### Key decisions made
- Do NOT sell raw milk. Use it as content/philosophy only.
- Do NOT make therapeutic claims of any kind.
- Do NOT fake scarcity or social proof.
- Do NOT spend on manufacturing before validation.
- DO validate with digital products and sampling before committing to a co-packer.
- Register food business with local council before selling anything.
- Complete Level 2 Food Hygiene Certificate before selling anything.
