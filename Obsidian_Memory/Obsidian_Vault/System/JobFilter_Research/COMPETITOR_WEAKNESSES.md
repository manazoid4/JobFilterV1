# Competitor Weaknesses

## Analysis Framework

For each competitor:
- Business model
- Trust weaknesses
- Emotional weaknesses
- UX weaknesses
- User complaints
- JobFilter counter-position

---

## Checkatrade

**Business Model:** Annual subscription (£600-1,200) + directory listing + optional lead boosts.

**Trust Weaknesses:**
- Reviews can be faked or filtered.
- "Guarantee" only covers work done, not lead quality.
- No verification of homeowner intent.
- Tradesmen pay regardless of results.

**Emotional Weaknesses:**
- Called "Check-a-prat" by tradesmen.
- Anger over "used all your leads" despite annual fee.
- Feels like paying for a badge, not jobs.

**UX Weaknesses:**
- Cluttered directory.
- Homeowners browse, not buy.
- No urgency signals.
- No territory protection.

**User Complaints:**
- "£1200 a year for leads that go nowhere."
- "Low-value enquiries, mostly timewasters."
- "Push you to buy more credits."

**JobFilter Counter:**
- No annual fee without results.
- Pay only when you get actionable signals.
- Exclusivity, not directory listing.
- Real data, not reviews.

---

## MyBuilder

**Business Model:** Commission (10-20% of job value) or shortlist fees.

**Trust Weaknesses:**
- Lead shared with multiple tradesmen.
- Homeowner chooses from shortlist — race to bottom.
- No pre-qualification.
- Platform takes cut regardless of margin.

**Emotional Weaknesses:**
- "MyTimeWaster" nickname.
- Anger over paying for leads that don't convert.
- Feeling of being just another quote.

**UX Weaknesses:**
- Homeowner-centric, not tradesman-centric.
- No signal intelligence.
- No territory focus.
- Dashboard feels like an afterthought.

**User Complaints:**
- "By the time I call, three others have quoted."
- "Homeowner just wanted the cheapest."
- "Commission eats my profit."

**JobFilter Counter:**
- No commission. Flat fee.
- Exclusive leads — no shortlist.
- Scored before you see them.
- Territory-locked.

---

## Bark

**Business Model:** Subscription + credit packs for lead access.

**Trust Weaknesses:**
- Leads sold to multiple users.
- Credit system feels like gambling.
- No refund for bad leads.
- Low barrier to entry = low quality.

**Emotional Weaknesses:**
- "Barking up the wrong tree."
- Frustration with credit burn rate.
- Feeling nickel-and-dimed.

**UX Weaknesses:**
- Credit system is confusing.
- Lead quality is inconsistent.
- No enrichment or scoring.

**User Complaints:**
- "Bought credits, leads were useless."
- "Same lead on Bark and Checkatrade."
- "Support doesn't care."

**JobFilter Counter:**
- No credits. Simple monthly.
- Every signal is enriched and scored.
- Official data, not user-submitted.
- Direct support via WhatsApp.

---

## Rated People

**Business Model:** Lead purchase (£10-50 per lead) + subscription tiers.

**Trust Weaknesses:**
- Lead shared among rated tradesmen.
- No verification of job urgency.
- Annual fee + per-lead cost = double dip.
- Review system gameable.

**Emotional Weaknesses:**
- "Paying to compete."
- Frustration with lead quality.
- Feeling like a number.

**UX Weaknesses:**
- Cluttered interface.
- No territory tools.
- No intelligence layer.

**JobFilter Counter:**
- Flat fee, no per-lead cost.
- Exclusive territory.
- Intelligence-first, not review-first.

---

## TrustATrader

**Business Model:** Directory subscription + review system.

**Trust Weaknesses:**
- Directory model = passive waiting.
- No proactive signals.
- Review-dependent, not data-dependent.

**JobFilter Counter:**
- Proactive alerts, not passive listing.
- Data-driven, not review-driven.

---

## Google Local Services

**Business Model:** Pay per lead.

**Trust Weaknesses:**
- Lead shared with multiple providers.
- No scoring or enrichment.
- Disputes are difficult.
- Generic, not construction-specific.

**JobFilter Counter:**
- Construction-specific signals.
- Scored and enriched.
- Exclusive, not shared.

---

## Glenigan / Barbour ABI / Planning Pipe

**Business Model:** Enterprise subscriptions (£5,000-50,000/year).

**Trust Weaknesses:**
- Too expensive for small trades.
- Data-heavy, not action-oriented.
- No territory exclusivity.
- No WhatsApp delivery.

**JobFilter Counter:**
- Affordable for single tradesmen.
- Action-oriented (WhatsApp, letters).
- Territory exclusivity.
- Scored, not raw data.

## Strategic Insight

No competitor combines:
1. Official data fusion
2. Territory exclusivity
3. Anti-ghost scoring
4. Affordable pricing
5. WhatsApp delivery

This is JobFilter's unassailable moat.
