# Brand Mythology and Positioning

## The Core Myth

JobFilter is not a software company.
JobFilter is a **construction intelligence operation** built by someone who was tired of watching good builders waste time.

---

## Brand Archetype

**The Operator / The Protector**

Not the visionary. Not the disruptor. The operator.

- Someone who has been on site.
- Someone who understands that time is money.
- Someone who protects tradesmen from the systems that exploit them.

## Tone of Voice

### Writing Rules

1. **Lead with the pain.**
   - "Wasted quotes cost you £3,000 a year."
   - Not: "JobFilter helps you find better leads."

2. **Use active voice.**
   - "We scan official sources."
   - Not: "Official sources are scanned."

3. **One idea per sentence.**
   - No compound sentences.
   - No "and" chains.

4. **Use tradesman language.**
   - "Quote", "site", "waste", "graft", "job", "patch".
   - Not: "Opportunity", "engagement", "optimisation".

5. **Be specific.**
   - "£42,000 extension in B17."
   - Not: "High-value job in your area."

6. **No exclamation marks.**
   - Excitement reads as desperation.

7. **No emojis.**
   - Period.

### Example Copy

**Weak:**
"JobFilter is an AI-powered lead generation platform for UK tradesmen."

**Strong:**
"JobFilter scans official construction data and sends scored leads to your WhatsApp. One trade partner per postcode."

**Weak:**
"Our innovative solution leverages machine learning to optimise your lead pipeline."

**Strong:**
"We check planning, EPC, and land registry. If the signal is strong, it reaches your phone. If it's weak, we bin it."

## Visual Identity

### Colour System
| Role | Colour | Hex | Usage |
|------|--------|-----|-------|
| Primary | JobFilter Yellow | #E3B72A | CTAs, highlights, badges |
| Background | Paper | #FFFDF4 | Page backgrounds |
| Ink | Black | #080808 | Text, borders |
| Muted | Dark Grey | #2F2F2A | Secondary text |
| Green | Signal Green | #22C55E | Verified, go, success |
| Orange | Caution | #C5462A | Warning, risk, alert |
| Steel | Industrial | #222629 | Dark sections |

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Barlow Condensed, uppercase, bold, tight leading.
- **Body:** Barlow, regular weight, comfortable leading.
- **Data:** JetBrains Mono, for numbers and scores.

### Imagery
- No stock photos of builders shaking hands.
- No fake smiles.
- Preferred: data visualisations, maps, construction sites, industrial textures.
- If people: real tradesmen, real sites, candid shots.

### UI Style
- Bold borders (2px+).
- Strong shadows (offset, not blur).
- Boxy, industrial feel.
- No rounded corners (or minimal).
- No gradients (or minimal).
