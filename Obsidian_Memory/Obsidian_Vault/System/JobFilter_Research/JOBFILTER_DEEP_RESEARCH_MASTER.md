# JobFilter Deep Research Master

## The Construction Intelligence Opportunity

### Market Context
UK construction is a £300bn+ sector with 2.7 million workers. Small tradesmen (1-10 employees) represent the majority of firms but have the worst tools for finding work. They rely on word-of-mouth, local reputation, and increasingly desperate attempts at digital marketing.

### The Trust Gap
Every existing platform fails the trust test:
- **Shared leads** create bidding wars that destroy margins.
- **Annual fees** are paid regardless of results.
- **Vague scoring** leaves tradesmen guessing.
- **No exclusivity** means the best jobs go to the fastest clicker.

### JobFilter's Unique Position
JobFilter does not sell leads. It sells **time protection** and **information advantage**.

## Core Insights

### 1. Tradesmen Language is Brutal and Honest
They do not use business jargon. They say:
- "Waste of time"
- "Ghosted after quote"
- " tyre-kicker"
- "Price shoppers"
- "Check-a-prat"

Any platform that sounds like a startup pitch deck loses them immediately.

### 2. The Emotional Journey of a Quote
1. **Hope** — new enquiry comes in.
2. **Effort** — drive, assess, write quote.
3. **Silence** — no response.
4. **Anger** — time and fuel wasted.
5. **Distrust** — platform gets blamed.

JobFilter must intervene at stage 1, not stage 5.

### 3. Serious Buyers Have Data Footprints
A homeowner who has:
- Submitted a planning application
- Registered a property purchase
- Applied for an HMO licence
- Listed a building control notice

...is infinitely more serious than someone posting "need a builder" on Facebook.

### 4. Territory Matters More Than Volume
A roofer in B12 does not want leads in SE15. They want **all** the roof-worthy signals in their patch, before anyone else sees them.

## Strategic Pillars

### Pillar 1: Trust Through Transparency
Show exactly how a lead is scored. Show the data sources (at category level, not API level). Explain the methodology.

### Pillar 2: Anti-Ghost Infrastructure
Score every lead for likelihood of follow-through. Flag high-risk enquiries before a tradesman wastes a visit.

### Pillar 3: Territory Exclusivity
One partner per postcode cluster per trade. No shared leads. No bidding wars.

### Pillar 4: Intelligence Authority
Weekly briefings, field notes, and tactical content that makes tradesmen smarter — not just busier.

### Pillar 5: Operator Presence
The brand must feel like it was built by someone who understands construction, not a tech bro in Shoreditch.

## Data Moat

The combination of:
- Planning applications
- EPC ratings
- Land Registry transactions
- Companies House registrations
- Building control notices
- HMO licensing
- Council contracts
- Auction results

...creates a signal graph that no single competitor has assembled.

**Key insight:** Each data source is free and publicly available. The moat is in the **fusion**, **scoring**, and **delivery speed** — not in proprietary data.
