1. The 60-Second Onboard

Goal: Auto-fill the tradie’s profile so they don't have to type.

    The Input: Tradie gives you their Website or Instagram.

    The Action: A scraper pulls their photos and "About" text.

    The AI: Vertex AI reads the data and identifies their niche (e.g., "Loft Specialist in Leeds").

    The Result: A WhatsApp to the tradie: "We’ve built your profile. Does this look right?"

2. The Planning Stealth-Listener

Goal: Get the lead before the homeowner even starts Googling.

    The Input: A script checks Local Council Portals for new planning apps.

    The Action: It pulls the project description and PDF blueprints.

    The AI: Gemini "reads" the drawings to see exactly what work is needed.

    The Result: A WhatsApp to the tradie: "New extension approved in [Postcode]. Likely needs a full rewire. Tap to claim."

3. The Visual Estimator

Goal: Use AI to "pre-inspect" the job via photo.

    The Input: Homeowner uploads a photo (e.g., an old fuse box).

    The Action: The photo is sent to Gemini Vision.

    The AI: Identifies the issue (e.g., "Outdated plastic unit, non-compliant").

    The Result: A WhatsApp to the tradie: "Gold Lead: Visual check confirms old unit. Estimated 1-day job. See photo."

4. The WhatsApp Gatekeeper

Goal: Block timewasters while the tradie is on a ladder.

    The Input: A new homeowner messages the tradie's WhatsApp.

    The Action: An AI Bot asks for the postcode, photos, and budget.

    The AI: Decides if the lead is "Gold" or a "Timewaster."

    The Result: The tradie’s phone only pings if the lead is Qualified.
