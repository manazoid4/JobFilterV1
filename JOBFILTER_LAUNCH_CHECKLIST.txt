=========================================================
JOBFILTER: FINAL LAUNCH CHECKLIST
=========================================================
Print this out or copy it to your notes. These are the exact 
keys you need to gather to make the site 100% real.

[ ] STEP 1: THE DATABASE (Firestore for waitlist, sessions, blueprint)
    1. Go to Firebase Console for project: jobfilter-uk.
    2. Enable Firestore Database (production mode).
    3. Create a Service Account key (JSON).
    4. Paste JSON into a single-line env var.
    -> Save this as your FIREBASE_SERVICE_ACCOUNT.

[ ] STEP 2: THE EMAIL (Resend for onboarding + waitlist alerts)
    1. Go to https://resend.com and create an account.
    2. Add/verify jobfilter.uk sending domain.
    3. Create an API key.
    -> Save this as your RESEND_API_KEY.

[ ] STEP 3: THE MONEY (For charging the £50 Priority Pass)
    1. Go to https://stripe.com and create an account.
    2. Go to the "Developers" tab -> "API keys".
    3. Reveal and copy the "Secret key".
    -> Save this as your STRIPE_SECRET_KEY.

[ ] STEP 4: THE AI FOREMAN (For WhatsApp integration)
    1. Go to https://developers.facebook.com and log in.
    2. Create an App -> Select "Other" -> Select "Business".
    3. Add the "WhatsApp" product to your app.
    4. Follow the setup to get your temporary/permanent Access Token.
    -> Save this as your WHATSAPP_API_TOKEN.

[ ] STEP 5: PLUG IT ALL INTO GOOGLE CLOUD
    1. Go to your Google Cloud Console (where you deployed the site).
    2. Go to "Cloud Run" and click on your "jobfilter" service.
    3. Click "Edit & Deploy New Revision".
    4. Scroll down to "Variables & Secrets".
    5. Add the variables you collected above:
       - FIREBASE_SERVICE_ACCOUNT
       - RESEND_API_KEY
       - STRIPE_SECRET_KEY
       - WHATSAPP_API_TOKEN
       - APP_URL
    6. Click "Deploy".

=========================================================
Once Step 5 is done, your app is fully alive and processing real data!
