# JobFilter Competitor Strategy Playbook

## Objective
Use competitor strengths without turning JobFilter into a bloated all-in-one tool.
Keep JobFilter positioned as the **intake/filtering foreman** that helps tradies:
1) qualify better jobs faster,
2) quote and follow up with less admin,
3) convert more of the right work.

---

## 1) Competitor Landscape (Grouped by Category)

### A) Marketing Agencies / Lead Gen Services
- WeBuildTrades
- Buildmyservice.co.uk
- Timetoscale

**What they do well**
- Pipeline generation and demand capture.
- Website + SEO + paid campaigns for lead volume.

**Common pain points**
- Leads are often mixed quality.
- Weak qualification layer before tradies spend time quoting.
- Long payback for small operators.

**JobFilter counter-position**
- "We don’t just get you leads — we stop you wasting time on bad ones."

### B) Job Management Platforms (FSM / CRM / Ops)
- tradifyhq.com
- servicebro.io
- Fergus (fergus.com)
- Workpal.app
- HaulTechOfficial

**What they do well**
- Scheduling, jobs, invoicing, team workflows, operations stack.

**Common pain points**
- Heavy onboarding.
- Too broad for solo/small crews who just need faster lead decisions.
- Conversion support often secondary to admin workflows.

**JobFilter counter-position**
- JobFilter handles the **front-door decision layer** before full job management.
- Integrate with FSM tools later instead of replacing them.

### C) AI Receptionist / AI Call Handling
- Trademore.uk (Checkatrade AI receptionist angle)
- Cyberstaff_AI
- TradeHalo

**What they do well**
- Call capture, missed-call recovery, booking automation.

**Common pain points**
- Captures calls but may not filter quote-worthiness deeply.
- Generic scripts can reduce trust if not trade-specific.

**JobFilter counter-position**
- Keep optional call-intake automation only if it improves qualification quality.
- Don’t become a generic receptionist app.

### D) Estimating / Quoting Specialists
- eaziquote
- mybuildestimate.co.uk

**What they do well**
- Faster estimate generation and quote templates.

**Common pain points**
- Quote speed improves, but bad-job filtering still weak.
- Users still burn time on wrong opportunities.

**JobFilter counter-position**
- Pair filtering + Smart Quoting so quoting effort is spent on high-probability jobs.

### E) Website Builder / AI Presence Tools
- hereismywork.com

**What they do well**
- Fast website creation and online presence.

**Common pain points**
- Website doesn’t solve lead qualification quality.

**JobFilter counter-position**
- Optional ecosystem partnership; not core product.

### F) Unclear / Emerging
- Symterra.co.uk (position unclear)

**Action**
- Verify actual product scope before reacting.

## 2) What JobFilter Should Copy, Improve, and Ship

## Principles
- No feature bloat.
- Ship in weeks, not quarters.
- Every feature must improve one of:
  - speed-to-decision,
  - conversion rate,
  - cash collection,
  - retention.

### Feature Set A — Build Now (0–30 days)

1. **Lead Quality Score + Reason Codes (core differentiation)**
   - Show a 0–100 score per lead with plain-English flags:
     - distance risk,
     - budget mismatch,
     - urgency mismatch,
     - scope clarity.
   - Why tradies love it: instant go/no-go.

2. **Trade-Specific Intake Profiles**
   - Electrician/plumber/builder/heating profiles with pre-set filter defaults.
   - Why tradies love it: no setup headache.

3. **Smart Quoting Fast-Start Packs** *(allowed paid feature)*
   - Prebuilt quote starters by trade + job type.
   - Why tradies love it: quote faster with less typing.

4. **Payment Chaser Smart Cadence** *(allowed paid feature)*
   - “Friendly → firm → final” reminder sequence templates.
   - Why tradies love it: better cashflow without awkward manual follow-up.

5. **Review Harvester Auto-Prompt Rules** *(allowed paid feature)*
   - Trigger requests after marked-complete jobs with one-click links.
   - Why tradies love it: more reviews, less admin.

### Feature Set B — Build Next (30–60 days)

6. **Missed-Call to Intake Capture (lightweight)**
   - If a call is missed, send intake link + capture minimal job details.
   - Keep as intake extension, not full call-center product.

7. **Quote-Win Feedback Loop**
   - Mark outcomes (won/lost/no quote) and feed back to lead scoring quality.
   - Why tradies love it: system gets smarter with their real jobs.

8. **One-Page Weekly Foreman Digest**
   - Jobs filtered, quotes sent, payments chased, reviews requested, quick action list.
   - Why tradies love it: one glance operational control.

### Feature Set C — Integrate, Don’t Rebuild (60+ days)

9. **FSM Integrations Instead of Full FSM Build**
   - Push qualified leads into Tradify/Fergus/ServiceM8-style workflows.
   - Keep JobFilter as the intelligence layer at intake.

10. **Marketing Source Attribution Lite**
   - Track best channels at intake source level without becoming an agency dashboard.

## 3) Problems to Avoid (Where Competitors Usually Fail)

1. Becoming a generic all-in-one suite that is slow and expensive.
2. Forcing heavy onboarding before value is shown.
3. Measuring lead volume instead of profitable lead quality.
4. Adding AI features with weak reliability and no operator control.
5. Ignoring cash-collection and review loops (retention killers).

---

## 4) Suggested Product Packaging (Simple + Monetizable)

### Free
- Intake scan
- Lead quality filtering view
- 3 full record opens/month

### Scout Pro (default)
- Unlimited full records
- Job alerts
- Smart Quoting
- Payment Chaser
- Review Harvester

### Hammer (optional add-on)
- Concierge outbound/intake support for operators who want done-for-you execution.

---

## 5) If This Should Be a Separate Business (Recommendation)

Only split if you want to go deep into full AI receptionist/call-center automation.

### Spinout idea: **ForemanPhone** (separate product)
- AI call handler + booking + voicemail rescue + dispatch handoff.
- Target users: larger service teams with high inbound call volume.
- Why separate: different operational complexity, support burden, and product DNA from JobFilter’s intake-filtering mission.

---

## 6) 90-Day Execution Sequence

### Phase 1 (Weeks 1–2)
- Implement lead quality score with reason flags.
- Add trade-specific intake defaults.
- Instrument KPIs.

### Phase 2 (Weeks 3–6)
- Launch Smart Quoting packs, Payment Chaser templates, Review Harvester rules.

### Phase 3 (Weeks 7–10)
- Add missed-call capture and quote-win feedback loop.

### Phase 4 (Weeks 11–13)
- Add weekly digest and integrate first FSM target.
